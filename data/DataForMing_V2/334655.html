N.Y. judge backs Apple in encryption fight - Apple Inc. (NASDAQ:AAPL) | Seeking Alpha Mar 1 2016, 03:42 ET | About: Apple Inc. (AAPL) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor The U.S. government cannot force Apple (NASDAQ: AAPL ) to unlock an iPhone in a New York drug case, U.S. Magistrate Judge James Orenstein declared on Monday, a ruling that could affect a similar decision involving the San Bernardino shooter's iPhone in California. "It would betray our constitutional heritage and our people’s claim to democratic governance for a judge to pretend that our Founders already had that debate, and ended it, in 1789,” Judge Orenstein wrote, referring to the archaic All Writs Act. The ruling, which will likely shape the broader battle about privacy and security, comes before today's congressional testimony on "San Bernardino" from Apple and the FBI.
