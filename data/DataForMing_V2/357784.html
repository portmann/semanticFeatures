More advances toward reusable rocket boosters - Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) | Seeking Alpha Apr 3 2016, 10:38 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Jeff Bezos' space transportation company Blue Origin has successfully launched and landed for the third time a suborbital rocket capable of carrying six passengers, increasing the pressure on Elon Musk's SpaceX. Bezos, the founder of Amazon.com (NASDAQ: AMZN ), said earlier this month that Blue Origin expects to begin crewed test flights of the New Shepard rocket next year and begin flying paying passengers as early as 2018.
