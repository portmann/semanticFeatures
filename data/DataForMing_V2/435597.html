Macau casino execs minimize impact of Shanghai Disney - SJM Holdings Ltd. (OTCMKTS:SJMHF) | Seeking Alpha Jun 17 2016, 08:41 ET | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor SJM Holdings ( OTCPK:SJMHF , OTCPK:SJMHY ) CEO Ambrose So Shu Fai says the opening of Disney's massive theme park in Shanghai doesn't pose a threat to Macau casinos and hotels due to the different set of targeted customers. The SJM chief's analysis differs from the conclusion of a recent report from Morgan Stanley that warned on the impact of Shanghai Disney Resort on traffic.
