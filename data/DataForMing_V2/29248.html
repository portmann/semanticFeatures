ETF Hub Apr 16 2015, 04:56 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor The jumbo jet may be slowly heading off the assembly line, after a slump in sales raised questions over how long manufacturers can sustain production. Last year, there were zero orders placed by commercial airlines for new Boeing (NYSE: BA ) 747s or Airbus ( OTCPK:EADSY ) A380s, reflecting a fundamental shift in the industry toward smaller, twin-engine planes. A sustained upturn in air freight traffic may secure the 747 a longer future, but the large availability of leases and cheap smaller planes may take its toll on production.
