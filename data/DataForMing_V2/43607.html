Apple finds defects with Apple watch - WSJ | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Cyclical Consumer Goods, Technology April 29 (Reuters) - Apple Inc has found defects with the Apple watch, the Wall Street Journal reported, citing sources. A Chinese-made component of the Apple Watch was found to be defective, causing the company to limit availability of the watch, the Journal said, citing people familiar with the matter.
