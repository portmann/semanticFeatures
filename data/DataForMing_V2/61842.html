ETF Hub May 19 2015, 09:27 ET | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor Shares of Netflix (NASDAQ: NFLX ) are trading at all-time highs in premarket trading with the price target bar from Wall Street getting boosted to a new level. Boutique investment firm Pivotal Research set its PT on the streamer at $850 after factoring in favorable China and South Korea penetration rates. A faster pace of international growth led Pivotal to move higher the EBITDA multiple it uses to extrapolate a price target.
