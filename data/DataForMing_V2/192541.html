Facebook to beam Internet from space - Facebook (NASDAQ:FB) | Seeking Alpha Oct 6 2015, 04:00 ET | About: Facebook (FB) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Forget about its Aquila drone, Facebook (NASDAQ: FB ) is getting serious about beaming Internet down to remote parts of the world. The company now plans to launch a satellite in partnership with France's Eutelsat Communications ( OTCPK:EUTLY ) to bring connectivity to large parts of sub-Saharan Africa.
