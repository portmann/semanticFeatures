Boeing eyes new partnerships, sees upward pressure on 737 output | Reuters DUBAIBoeing expects to announce new industrial partnerships at the Dubai Airshow, executives said at a news conference on Saturday. The U.S. planemaker also sees "upward pressure" in single-aisle commercial jet production rates. Boeing currently plans to raise output of its 737 model to 52 a month in 2018.
