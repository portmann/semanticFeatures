WSJ: Hulu developing cable-style online TV service - The Walt Disney Company (NYSE:DIS) | Seeking Alpha May 2 2016, 04:49 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Hulu is developing a subscription service that would stream feeds of popular broadcast and cable TV channels, WSJ reports , in a move that would make the company a competitor to traditional pay-TV providers and other new digital entrants. The company, co-owned by Disney (NYSE: DIS ), Comcast (NASDAQ: CMCSA ) and Fox ( FOX , FOXA ) hopes to launch the new online service in the first quarter of 2017.
