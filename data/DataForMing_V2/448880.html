Digital driving gains for Disney in upfront ad talks - The Walt Disney Company (NYSE:DIS) | Seeking Alpha Jul 1 2016, 19:15 ET | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor In ad negotiations, Walt Disney ( DIS +0.2%) has seen nice increases in its upfronts, thanks to digital video . Buyers are purchasing video on all screens, the company tells Jon Lafayette, in an environment where "Our kids have grown up multiscreen." Dollar volume was up 20% overall, with linear TV sales up 11%. TV pricing was up in the high-single-digits to low-double-digits. The Mouse House got sponsors for two new apps: short-form content app Disney LOL and short-form video app Mickey Video.
