ETF Hub Dec 15 2015, 04:32 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor The Force Awakens, the first film from Disney (NYSE: DIS ) in the Star Wars series, will debut in more than 4,100 theaters this weekend, a record number for a December opening. BoxOffice.com is forecasting $223M in U.S. and Canadian ticket sales in the first weekend, topping Jurassic World from Universal Pictures, which brought in $208.8M in its debut weekend in June.
