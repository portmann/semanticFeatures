Boeing wins $11.3B order from VietJet - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha May 23 2016, 02:29 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Vietnam's VietJet has agreed to order 100 Boeing (NYSE: BA ) 737 MAX 200 airplanes, in a deal worth $11.3B based on list prices. Delivery of the planes will run for four years beginning in 2019, and will make the airline one of the fastest growing low-cost carriers in the region. What a vote of confidence in a far brighter diplomatic and reestablishment of economic ties between the United States and Vietnam. Great news for Boeing as well!
