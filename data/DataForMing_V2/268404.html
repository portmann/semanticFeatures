(AAPL) | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor Clearing a major hurdle, Apple (NASDAQ: AAPL ) and China UnionPay are teaming up to bring Apple Pay to China "as soon as early 2016," the companies announced. China UnionPay holds a monopoly on credit and debit processing in the country, previously considered a major headwind to Apple's plans for the country (which also features private payments by Alibaba's Alipay and Tencent's WeChat). In the plan, the company's cardholders will be able to add bank cards to Apple Pay on iOS devices. The solution will utilize China UnionPay's QuickPass ars part of the security for the system.
