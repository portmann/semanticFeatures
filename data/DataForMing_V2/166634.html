Yahoo says IRS denies ruling on tax-free Alibaba spinoff | Reuters Sept 8 Yahoo Inc said the Internal Revenue Service had denied the company's request for a private letter ruling on whether the spinoff of its stake in Alibaba Group Holdings Ltd would be tax free. The board would continue to carefully consider the company's options, including proceeding with the spinoff on the basis of an opinion of its counsel, Yahoo said.
