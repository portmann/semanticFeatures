Apple could pursue details about FBI iPhone hack - Apple Inc. (NASDAQ:AAPL) | Seeking Alpha Mar 30 2016, 02:59 ET | About: Apple Inc. Questions are still lingering over how the FBI found a backdoor into the device, but the agency reportedly hired Israeli-firm Cellebrite for technical assistance in accessing the phone.
