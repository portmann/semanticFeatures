Importance of video growing at Facebook - Facebook (NASDAQ:FB) | Seeking Alpha Jun 25 2015, 04:40 ET | About: Facebook (FB) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Amid a big push into the video space, Facebook (NASDAQ: FB ) is streaming the first episodes of two new HBO (NYSE: TWX ) comedy series for the first time. The episodes of "Ballers" and "The Brink" will be hosted on the social network for a limited time, following their June 21 debuts on TV. According to Ampere Analysis, Facebook is expected to exceed two trillion video views this year – that's two thirds of YouTube's projected figure for the same period.
