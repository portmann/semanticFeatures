Disney revenue rises 5.1 percent | Reuters Aug 4 Media company Walt Disney Co's revenue rose 5.1 percent in the third quarter as its TV networks pulled in higher fees and theme parks attracted more visitors. Revenue rose to $13.10 billion in the quarter ended June 27 from $12.47 billion a year earlier, the company said on Tuesday. Net income climbed to $2.48 billion, or $1.45 per share, from $2.25 billion, or $1.28 per share.
