A new mid-sized jetliner from Boeing? - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Jul 1 2016, 05:24 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor After a year of meetings with potential customers, Boeing (NYSE: BA ) is advancing toward developing a new jetliner with up to 270 seats that would target mid-range flights of up to 10 hours. A new larger version of the 737 Max is also being considered, and according to Boeing marketing chief Randy Tinseth, doing both new planes remains an option.
