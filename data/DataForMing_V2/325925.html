Verizon to buy XO Communications' fiber-optic business | Reuters The No-1 U.S. wireless phone provider Verizon Communications Inc said it would buy XO Communications' fiber-optic network business for about $1.8 billion. Verizon on Monday said it expected the net present value of the operational synergies to be more than $1.5 billion.
