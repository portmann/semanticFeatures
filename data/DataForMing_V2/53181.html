ETF Hub May 7 2015, 19:43 ET | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor Disney (NYSE: DIS ) has named Leslie Ferraro its president of Consumer Products , effective immediately. Ferraro comes out of the Parks & Resorts group where she led marketing and sales teams in campaign development. She joined Disney in 1999, and will report to CEO Bob Iger and COO Thomas Staggs. In Tuesday's earnings report, Disney noted strong fiscal second-quarter growth in consumer products (revenues that grew 10% to $971M, and operating income up 32% to $362M), and the division has big opportunities ahead as the company plans merchandising for a wide range of Star Wars films as well as years of scheduled Marvel films -- not to mention the continuing success that is merchandise tied to Frozen.
