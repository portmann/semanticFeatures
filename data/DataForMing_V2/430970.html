Facebook activates Safety Check for first time in U.S. - Facebook (NASDAQ:FB) | Seeking Alpha Jun 13 2016, 03:26 ET | About: Facebook (FB) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Facebook (NASDAQ: FB ) has turned on its "Safety Check" for the first time in the U.S. following the weekend shooting in Orlando . The feature allows people to use the social networking site or app to alert people and friends that they are safe in the wake of a crisis or disaster.
