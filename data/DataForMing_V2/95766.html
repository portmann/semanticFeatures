Ford joins car-sharing business - Ford Motor Company (NYSE:F) | Seeking Alpha Jun 24 2015, 03:38 ET | About: Ford Motor Company (F) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Ford (NYSE: F ) is jumping into the car-sharing market, launching a pilot program in six U.S. cities and London, England. The new program will enable owners of vehicles financed by Ford Motor Credit to offer their car, SUV or truck to pre-screened clients for short-term rentals.
