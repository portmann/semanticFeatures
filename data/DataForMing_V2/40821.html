Verizon says CBS Sports Network will be available on 'Custom TV' | Reuters NEW YORK, April 27 (Reuters) - Verizon Communications said on Monday that content from CBS Corp's Sports Network will be available on its new pay TV offering starting May 1. CBS Sports Network has joined Verizon FiOS's new Custom TV lineup under its sports package, a Verizon spokesman told Reuters.
