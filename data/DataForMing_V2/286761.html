ETF Hub Jan 11 2016, 16:47 ET | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor Walt Disney's ( DIS +0.7%) board has elected Nike president/CEO Mark Parker as a director . Parker's appointment brings the board to 12 members. He'll stand for election with the other directors on March 3 at Disney's annual meeting. Parker has held the leadership posts at Nike since January 2006 after rising to it from his original job as footwear designer. His "keen insight into consumers and his broad experience in international markets make him a great fit for the Disney Board," says Disney chief Robert Iger.
