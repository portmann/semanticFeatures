Boeing raises share buyback authorization to $14 bln | Reuters Dec 14 Boeing Co raised its share repurchase authorization to $14 billion from $12 billion and also increased its quarterly dividend. The planemaker, which had $5.25 billion remaining under the $12 billion buyback plan, said it raised its dividend to $1.09 per share.
