ETF Hub Dec 3 2015, 20:04 ET | About: Verizon Communications (VZ) | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor Verizon ( VZ -0.8%) today offered 2 GB of bonus data for new phones added or upgraded on its larger data buckets (XL or XXL, in the company's clothing-size parlance). The deal is good just through Jan. 6. The data's shareable with devices on the plan, but tablets/connected devices alone aren't eligible to be added/upgraded for the deal. Meanwhile, T-Mobile's ( TMUS -3.3%) target of the day is AT&T ( T -1.4%); it's offering those customers an iPhone bargain (128 GB model for the price of the 16 GB model) and half off financed in-store accessories (focused on speakers, headphones, smartwatches, fitness trackers). T-Mobile says the iPhone deal represents a $200 value, given back via bill credit. Its deal is available Dec. 4 through Dec. 13.
