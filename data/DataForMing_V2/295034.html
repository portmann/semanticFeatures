Apple seeks India government approval to set up retail stores | Reuters MUMBAI Apple Inc (AAPL.O) has filed an application with the Indian government to set up its retail outlets in one of the fastest-growing smartphone markets, a spokesman for the Cupertino-based company said on Wednesday. Apple's retail expansion plans in India come at a time when concerns about slowing growth in the United States and China, the world's most important market for smartphones, have weighed on the company's stock in the last few months. The company currently sells its iPhones, iPads and Macs in India through third-party resellers.
