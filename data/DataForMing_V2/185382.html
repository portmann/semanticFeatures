Facebook.com down for some users | Reuters Sept 28 Facebook Inc's social media website was inaccessible for some users on Monday afternoon, its second outage in less than a week. A message displayed on facebook.com said "Sorry, something went wrong. We're working on it and we'll get it fixed as soon as we can."
