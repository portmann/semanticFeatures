Disney revenue rises 9 pct as ad sales rise | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Earnings, Cyclical Consumer Goods Nov 5 Media company Walt Disney Co reported a 9 percent rise in quarterly revenue as it earned more from ads on its cable TV networks including ESPN and as more people visited its U.S. theme parks. Revenue rose to $13.51 billion in the fourth quarter ended Oct. 3 from $12.39 billion a year earlier, the company said on Thursday. Net income climbed to $1.61 billion, or 95 cents per share, from $1.50 billion, or 86 cents per share, a year earlier.
