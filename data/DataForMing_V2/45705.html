Apple eyes yen-denominated bond | Reuters LONDON, May 1 (IFR) - Apple will start calls with fixed-income investors from May 7 ahead of a potential yen-denominated bond deal, according to a lead. The US company, rated Aa1 by Moody's and AA+ by Standard & Poor's, has hired Goldman Sachs and MUFG to arrange the calls.
