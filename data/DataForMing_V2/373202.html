Now, Facebook is rolling out "Facebook at Work" for corporate intranet websites. This will compete with LinkedIn (NYSE: LNKD ), Microsoft's (NASDAQ: MSFT ) Yammer, Slack, and Jive (NASDAQ: JIVE ) and could be a threat to each. Jive has frequently been left at the altar - IBM (NYSE: IBM ) looked and passed in 2012... SAP (NYSE: SAP ) looked and passed in 2013... and Oracle (NYSE: ORCL ) looked and passed in 2014. So it may be on its own to compete with a company 1,000x its scale. Airgas (NYSE: ARG ) received CFIUS clearance for its acquisition by Air Liquide ( OTCPK:AIQUY ). The merger arbitrage spread offers a 5% annual return if the deal closes by midyear. Williams (NYSE: WMB ) issued a notice of redemption to holders of the company's 5.50% Junior Subordinated Convertible Debentures due 2033. The company will redeem all $141,400 of the aggregate principal amount of the outstanding debentures for cash on April 11.
