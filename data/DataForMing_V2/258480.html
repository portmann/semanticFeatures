ETF Hub Dec 7 2015, 19:34 ET | About: Verizon Communications (VZ) | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor With a hot holiday season ahead, Verizon (NYSE: VZ ) has surpassed AT&T (NYSE: T ) as the wireless industry's top advertising spender for November. Of a total $189.3M placing TV commercials, Verizon spent about $1.2M more than AT&T, for about 21.1% of the total, FierceWireless/iSpot.tv say. Those two were ahead of T-Mobile (NASDAQ: TMUS ). Meanwhile, Cricket Wireless, AT&T's prepaid brand, was the fourth-largest spender, putting big-four member Sprint (NYSE: S ) in fifth place -- with about half of its spending going to its new half-off promotion.
