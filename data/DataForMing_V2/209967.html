ETF Hub Oct 20 2015, 16:47 ET | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor ESPN (NYSE: DIS ) is set to lay off up to 350 employees , with details coming as soon as tomorrow, Bloomberg reports. Observers began to expect some layoffs last month amid reports that Disney told the network to cut $100M from the coming year's budget, and another $250M from 2017. That followed a disappointing quarterly earnings report from Disney where CEO Bob Iger gave a lot of airtime to subscriber losses.
