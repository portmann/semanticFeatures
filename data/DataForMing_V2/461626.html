Microsoft Xbox One S scheduled for release - Microsoft Corporation (NASDAQ:MSFT) | Seeking Alpha Jul 18 2016, 15:44 ET | About: Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) | By: Eric McCaffrey , SA News Editor The Xbox One S, a console 40% smaller than the existing Xbox One, is set for release by Microsoft ( MSFT +0.5%) on August 2.  Pre-orders are now being processed. This redesigned and slimmer system will have support for 4K and HDR video, and will include a newly modified Xbox Wireless Controller. Only a 2TB model is presently available to preorder at $399. 500GB and 1TB models are planned for release in December at price points of $299 and $349, respectively.
