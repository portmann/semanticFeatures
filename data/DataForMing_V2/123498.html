Apple says App Store, Apple Music services facing disruptions | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Cyclical Consumer Goods, Technology July 21 Apple Inc said it is experiencing some issues with its App Store, Apple Music, iTunes Store and some other services. "We are investigating and will update the status as more information becomes available," Apple said on its services support page.
