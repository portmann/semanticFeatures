WhatsApp joins the billion users club - Facebook (NASDAQ:FB) | Seeking Alpha Feb 2 2016, 04:05 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor After buying WhatsApp two years ago in a deal worth about $22B, Facebook (NASDAQ: FB ) founder Mark Zuckerberg said he expects the messaging app to contribute to the company's bottom line after it hits roughly a billion users. That metric has now been reached . The firm also stated recently it was exploring new WhatsApp business models geared toward profitability and scrapped the service's $0.99 annual renewal fee. If an APP created by 14 people in 2 years, can achieve 1bn penetration in 7 years, doesn't that mean that there is ABSOLUTELY no moat in this market ?
