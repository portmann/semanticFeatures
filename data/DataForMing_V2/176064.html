Verizon roams into Cuba - Verizon Communications (NYSE:VZ) | Seeking Alpha Sep 18 2015, 02:48 ET | About: Verizon Communications (VZ) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Verizon (NYSE: VZ ) will begin offering roaming wireless service in Cuba next week, becoming the first U.S. company to do so. Currently, visiting Americans must purchase plans through state-owned Empresa de Telecomunicaciones de Cuba to have cellular service on the island, or have a cellphone account in a third country. The news comes on the same day Cuba said its first ambassador to the U.S. since 1961 presented his credentials at the White House.
