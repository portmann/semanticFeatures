Yahoo to shutter several services - Yahoo! Inc. (NASDAQ:YHOO) | Seeking Alpha Jun 5 2015, 02:27 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Yahoo (NASDAQ: YHOO ) is shutting down a few of its services, including its Yahoo Maps site, as it realigns itself to focus on search and digital content. Yahoo Pipes is on schedule to go into read-only mode (no Pipes creation) on August 30, and API GeoPlanet and PlaceSpotter will meet their demise. Yahoo also highlighted it will no longer support older versions of Apple's iPhone operating system and several regional media sites.
