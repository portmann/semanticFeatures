Yahoo to proceed with Alibaba spinoff as planned | Reuters Related: Stocks, M&A, Markets, Cyclical Consumer Goods Sept 28 Yahoo Inc said it would proceed with the planned spinoff of its stake in Alibaba Group Holding Ltd regardless of a favorable ruling from the U.S. Internal Revenue Service (IRS). The spin-off will continue to be subject to certain other conditions, including the receipt of a legal opinion on the tax-free treatment of the deal under U.S. federal tax laws, Yahoo said in a regulatory filing on Monday.
