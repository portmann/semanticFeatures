Yahoo annual meeting on tap - Yahoo! Inc. (NASDAQ:YHOO) | Seeking Alpha Jun 30 2016, 05:09 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Media leaks over who could buy Yahoo (NASDAQ: YHOO ) have died down ahead of the company's annual meeting today, where investors will likely ask for an update on the sales process. Shareholders are expected to approve Yahoo's new board of directors, which in April was expanded to 11 from nine members, and drill executives about the web portal's slow progress in selling its core internet business (final bids are due in early July).
