Boeing wins $714 million deal for more CH-47 helicopters | Reuters WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Boeing Co (BA.N) has been awarded a contract valued at $714 million for additional work on CH-47 helicopters for the U.S. Army, the U.S. Defense Department said on Friday. The contract, which runs through Dec. 31, 2020, covers 26 remanufactured CH-47F helicopters, four new CH-47s, and includes an option for two additional helicopters, the Pentagon said in its daily digest of major arms contracts. The contract, a modification of an existing contract with Boeing, also includes funding for parts and supplies with a long lead time, according to the notice.
