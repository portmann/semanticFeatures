Disney's second-quarter revenue rises 4.1 percent | Reuters Walt Disney Co's (DIS.N) reported a 4.1 percent rise in quarterly revenue, helped by the box-office success of "Star Wars: The Force Awakens" and animated movie "Zootopia". The net income attributable to company rose to $2.14 billion, or $1.30 per share, in the second quarter ended April 2, from $2.11 billion, or $1.23 per share, a year earlier. Revenue rose to $12.97 billion from $12.46 billion.
