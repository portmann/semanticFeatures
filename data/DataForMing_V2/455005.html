Yahoo lowered at Pivotal Research - Yahoo! Inc. (NASDAQ:YHOO) | Seeking Alpha Jul 11 2016, 08:48 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Pivotal Research downgraded Yahoo (NASDAQ: YHOO ) to Hold from Buy, based in part on declining market share in digital advertising. "Going the other direction in terms of market share, Yahoo - which peaked at around 20% share of global digital advertising between 2004 and 2006 - has around 3% share and falling," analyst Brian Wieser wrote in a research note.
