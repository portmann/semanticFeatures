AIRSHOW-China's Minsheng commits to buying 30 Boeing 737 jets | Reuters PARIS, June 16 U.S. planemaker Boeing said on Tuesday that China's Minsheng Financial Leasing had signed a memorandum of understanding to buy 30 of its 737 single-aisle jets. Boeing said at the Paris air show that Minsheng would take a mix of next-generation 737 and 737 MAX planes. The aircraft would be worth around $3 billion at list prices.
