Microsoft modifies focus in China - Microsoft Corporation (NASDAQ:MSFT) | Seeking Alpha May 10 2016, 05:03 ET | About: Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Microsoft (NASDAQ: MSFT ) is shutting down its Chinese Web portal next month as the company shifts its focus from providing online content to offering software and services for Windows 10 devices. The software giant, which is under investigation by Chinese antitrust authorities, said it plans to continue to invest in other business in the country as well as maintaining its research and development operations there.
