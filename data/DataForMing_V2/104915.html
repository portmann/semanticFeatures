ETF Hub Jul 2 2015, 09:44 ET | About: Spherix Incorporated (SPEX) | By: Eric Jhonsa , SA News Editor Spherix ( SPEX -1%) states the Eastern Virginia federal court handling its infringement suit against Verizon issued a Markman Order "interpreting certain key claims" in Verizon's favor for the sole patent at issue in the case. As a result, Verizon's motion for summary judgment was granted. CEO Anthony Hayes: "We are disappointed by the judge's decision in this case.  At this stage, we are reviewing the Court's decision, analyzing the appealable issues and considering all our options." The company notes it's asserting 20 other patents in different cases, and that the infringement claims asserted in a second suit against Verizon weren't affected.
