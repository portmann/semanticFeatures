UPDATE 1-Yahoo's media head Kathy Savitt to leave company | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Cyclical Consumer Goods, Technology Sept 11 (Reuters) - Yahoo Inc's media head, Kathy Savitt, has decided to leave, a company spokeswoman said. Yahoo is planning a major shakeup in its media unit and Savitt's job had been the topic of internal discussion for a while, technology website Re/code reported, citing sources. ( on.recode.net/1VRIwBQ ) At Yahoo, Savitt spearheaded the company's brand marketing and audience engagement strategies, as well as editorial and video content across Yahoo News, Yahoo Finance and Yahoo Sports. Savitt, who is also Yahoo's chief marketing officer, will report to STX Chief Executive Robert Simonds.
