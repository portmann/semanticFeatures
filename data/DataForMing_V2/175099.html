Verizon says 2016 earnings may plateau | Reuters Sept 17 Verizon Communications Inc said its 2016 earnings may "plateau" at 2015 levels due to near-term impacts including commercial model change in wireless. The company also cited the planned sale of its wireline business to Frontier Communications Corp as a reason for the lackluster forecast.
