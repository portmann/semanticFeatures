Alphabet surpasses Apple, now biggest traded US company | Reuters Feb 1 Alphabet Inc surpassed Apple Inc as the most valuable company in the United States in after-hours trading on Monday, knocking the iPhone maker from the top spot that it has held for the better part of four years. After the bell, Alphabet's combined share classes were worth $568 billion, compared with Apple, which had a value of about $535 billion.
