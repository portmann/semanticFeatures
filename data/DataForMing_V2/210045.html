Disney's ESPN planning to cut up to 350 jobs - Bloomberg | Reuters Oct 20 Walt Disney Co's sports network ESPN is planning to cut up to 350 jobs, Bloomberg reported, citing people with knowledge of the matter. The job cuts would be announced to employees as early as Wednesday, Bloomberg said on Tuesday. ( bloom.bg/1NSIBEG ) The job cuts come after Disney said in August it expects lower revenue due to a decline in subscribers and that ESPN had experienced "modest" subscriber losses as customers increasingly prefer digital platforms for entertainment.
