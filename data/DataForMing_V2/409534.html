(NASDAQ:AAPL) | Seeking Alpha Rangeley Capital portfolio managers Andrew Walker and Chris DeMuth Jr. host a 15-minute podcast, which you can subscribe to on audioBoom . Today, we discuss Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway (NYSE: BRK.A ) (NYSE: BRK.B ) and its new billion-dollar stake in Apple (NASDAQ: AAPL ). Then, we turn to Mr. Buffett's participation in the auction for Yahoo! (NASDAQ: YHOO ). We finish off with one of my recurring marital disputes involving price discipline in auctions.
