ETF Hub Jun 24 2015, 16:33 ET | About: Local Corporation (LOCM) | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor Local search and marketing firm Local Corp. ( LOCM -75%) has filed for bankruptcy after a steep drop in traffic left it shaky. Officials plan to sell the company, and may draw interest for its patent portfolio regarding searching in a geographic area. A drop in visitors last year meant it took in less in receipts than expected from Google and Yahoo, and the cash shortage caught the company off guard. Yahoo terminated its agreement with the company just last week.
