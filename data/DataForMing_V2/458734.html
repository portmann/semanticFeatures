Microsoft wins foreign email privacy appeal - Microsoft Corporation (NASDAQ:MSFT) | Seeking Alpha Jul 14 2016, 13:08 ET | About: Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) | By: Eric McCaffrey , SA News Editor A federal appeals court has overturned a 2014 order requiring Microsoft ( MSFT +0.5%) to hand over materials stored on servers in Dublin. Microsoft is one of the first companies in the U.S. to dispute demands to turn over information stored abroad with briefs filed in support by multiple peers ranging from Apple and Cisco to Gannett and Verizon.
