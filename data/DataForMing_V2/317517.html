Amazon acquires Italy-based software firm | Reuters Amazon Web Services, the cloud computing arm of Amazon.com Inc (AMZN.O), said it has acquired NICE, a software developer for technical computing. The company said it has signed an agreement with NICE, which is also a cloud computing firm, and expects the deal to close in the first quarter of 2016. ( amzn.to/1QZt7NP ) Italy-based NICE has customers in industries ranging from aerospace to industrial, energy and utilities.
