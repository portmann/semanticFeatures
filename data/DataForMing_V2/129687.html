ETF Hub Jul 27 2015, 12:51 ET | About: Best Buy Co. (BBY) | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor Shares of Best Buy ( BBY -1.3%) trade lower than broad market averages on a muted reaction to the company announcing it will be the first retail chain to sell Apple Watches. The company won't be selling the higher-end Apple Watch Edition among the 16 models it will offer. Analysts note the Apple Watch will only be available at roughly 17% of Best Buy's stores which will make it harder to move the needle.
