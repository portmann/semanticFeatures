Microsoft confirms Finnish phone site closure and job cuts | Reuters HELSINKI Microsoft Corp (MSFT.O) on Friday confirmed it will close Nokia Oyj's (NOKIA.HE) former handset product development unit in Salo, Finland, and will cut a total of up to 2,300 jobs in the Nordic country. The cuts, initially announced in July, are part of Microsoft's plan to cut 7,800 jobs globally, most from the phone hardware business that it bought from Nokia last year.
