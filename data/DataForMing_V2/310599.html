Apple ordered to pay $625 mln in patent dispute with VirnetX | Reuters NEW YORK Feb 3 Apple Inc must pay VirnetX Holding Corp more than $625 million for using the patent licensing company's Internet security technology without permission in its FaceTime and iMessage features, a federal jury in Texas said on Wednesday. The award was more than the $532 million VirnetX had asked for going into the trial, which began on Jan. 25. The jury said Apple's infringement was willful.
