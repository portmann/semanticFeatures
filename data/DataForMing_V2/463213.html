'Bad PR', Lack Of Programming Hurts Netflix In Q2 - Netflix, Inc. (NASDAQ:NFLX) | Seeking Alpha Analysis from Seeking Alpha's The Simplistic Investor points to several issues in Q2. Netflix (NASDAQ: NFLX ) was in the spotlight on Cheddar TV today, where they cited analysis from Seeking Alpha contributor The Simplistic Investor , who pointed to the company's sound miss on subscriber estimates and higher-than-expected churn rates due to price increases as reasons for its less-than-stellar Q2 earnings report. Cheddar said Netflix blames "bad PR" for its subscriber woes, and that original programming was weak for the content provider this quarter, thus, the stock got "crushed" post-earnings.
