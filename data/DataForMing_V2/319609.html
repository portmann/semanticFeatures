China's ICBC says will start accepting Apple Pay on Feb 18 | Reuters BEIJING Feb 16 Apple Inc's Apple Pay mobile payment system will be available in China for Industrial and Commercial Bank of China Ltd customers from Feb. 18, spokespeople for the bank said in a social media post. China would be Apple Pay's fifth country. Apple had previously said the system would launch in the country in early 2016.
