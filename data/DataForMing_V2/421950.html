Morgan Stanley clips Melco Crown on Disney threat and Studio City slowness - Melco Crown Entertainment Limited (NASDAQ:MPEL) | Seeking Alpha Jun 2 2016, 11:58 ET | About: Melco Crown Entertainment... (MPEL) | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor Morgan Stanley downgrades Melco Crown ( MPEL -2%) to Equalweight from Overweight on a dimmed view of the upside potential for the casino stock. A survey conducted by MS indicated that Melco's Studio City was less preferred by Chinese gaming customers. There's also a Disney factor in play after some respondents indicated that Shanghai Disney will pull them away from Macau casinos.
