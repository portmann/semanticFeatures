Yahoo extends deadline for bids by a week - Re/code | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Cyclical Consumer Goods, Technology April 8 Yahoo Inc has extended the deadline to bid for its businesses by a week to April 18, technology news website Re/code reported, citing people familiar with the matter. Yahoo had set an April 11 deadline for preliminary bids, which could yield a deal by June or July, the Wall Street Journal has reported. Verizon Communications Inc is ready to make a bid for Yahoo's Web business, and hopes to make a merger more successful by also making an offer for a stake in Yahoo Japan Corp , Bloomberg reported on Thursday. Yahoo was not immediately available for comment. The company's shares were up 1.2 percent in premarket trading on Friday.
