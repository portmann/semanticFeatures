Target confirms bustling Cyber Monday traffic - Target Corporation (NYSE:TGT) | Seeking Alpha Dec 1 2015, 12:34 ET | About: Target Corporation (TGT) | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor Target ( TGT +0.2%) confirms in a new blog post that Cyber Monday was its busiest e-commerce day in history. Orders on Target.com came in at twice the rate of its busiest day ever for the early part of the day. Specific traffic and sales figures weren't disclosed. U.S. e-commerce sales have been strong for Target in recent quarters, although it hasn't matched the pace generated by Amazon.
