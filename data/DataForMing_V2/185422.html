Facebook.com back in service after second outage | Reuters Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg speaks on stage during a town hall with Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi at Facebook's headquarters in Menlo Park, California September 27, 2015. Facebook Inc (FB.O) restored access to its social media website for most users on Monday afternoon, after its second outage in less than a week. According to Currentlydown.com, Facebook was down for about 42 minutes between 3 p.m. and 4 p.m. ET. ( bit.ly/1LhwMao ) “We are currently restoring Facebook services that people had trouble accessing earlier today due to a configuration," Facebook spokesman Jay Nancarrow said. Facebook's map on Downdetector.com, which monitors disruptions, showed major outages over parts of North America. ( bit.ly/1aIqOPd ) Facebook suffered a similar outage on Thursday when it was down in North America, Europe, Australia and India.
