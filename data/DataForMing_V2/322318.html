Amazon.com to open new fulfillment centre in UK | Reuters LONDON Feb 18 Amazon.com said on Thursday it planned to open a new fulfillment centre in Britain, creating 500 new jobs over three years, as the e-commerce company expands its European business. The jobs are part of Amazon's plans to create 2,500 jobs in the United Kingdom this year.
