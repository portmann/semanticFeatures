Apple, FBI to testify before House panels - Apple Inc. (NASDAQ:AAPL) | Seeking Alpha Apr 19 2016, 03:58 ET | About: Apple Inc. (AAPL) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor The battle over encryption is heading to Capitol Hill again as Apple's (NASDAQ: AAPL ) general counsel, Bruce Sewell, and FBI officials appear before House members to defend their positions. The company's court battle with the government over a phone used by one of the San Bernardino shooters was recently resolved, but the two parties continue to fight over unlocking an iPhone used in a New York drug case.
