Microsoft reports $3.2 bln quarterly loss | Reuters July 21 Microsoft Corp reported a $3.2 billion quarterly net loss, hurt by charges related to its Nokia phone business and job cuts, and weak demand for its Windows operating system. The company recorded impairment, integration and restructuring expenses of $8.4 billion in the fourth quarter ended June 30. The company reported net income of $4.61 billion, or 55 cents per share, a year earlier.
