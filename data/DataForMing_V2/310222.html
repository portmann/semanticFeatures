Mall CEO walks back his Amazon bookstore plans claim | Reuters General Growth Properties Inc (GGP.N) said on Wednesday that a statement concerning Amazon.com Inc (AMZN.O) made by its chief executive, Sandeep Mathrani, was not intended to represent Amazon's plans. Mathrani, responding to an analyst's question on a conference call, said on Tuesday that Amazon planned to open 300 to 400 bookstores.
