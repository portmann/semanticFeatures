More records for 'Finding Dory' - The Walt Disney Company (NYSE:DIS) | Seeking Alpha Jul 11 2016, 04:55 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Finding Dory has finally gotten knocked out of the top spot at the box office after three blockbuster weeks, but the success of new champion The Secret Life of Pets wasn't enough to keep the popular fish story from breaking more records. With another $20.3M over the weekend, Finding Dory is now the highest-grossing film of 2016 in U.S. theaters with $422.5M, and the highest-grossing animated movie of all time (domestically) for Walt Disney (NYSE: DIS ).
