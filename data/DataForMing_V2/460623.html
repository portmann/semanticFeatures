Boeing celebrates centennial with bold plans - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Jul 17 2016, 06:11 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Boeing (NYSE: BA ) marked its centennial on Friday with plans to sharpen its focus on innovation, including ambitious projects for supersonic commercial flight and a rocket that could carry humans to Mars. But innovation at Boeing will be "disciplined" and not endanger the future of the world's biggest planemaker, CEO Dennis Muilenburg told reporters at an event marking the company's founding on July 15, 1916.
