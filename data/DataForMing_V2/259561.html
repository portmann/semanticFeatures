Yahoo not to spin off Alibaba stake - CNBC | Reuters Dec 8 Yahoo Inc will not move forward with a spin-off of its stake in Alibaba Group Holding Ltd , CNBC reported on Tuesday, citing sources. Yahoo will examine a spin-off of its core business and is expected to announce the move on Wednesday, CNBC added.
