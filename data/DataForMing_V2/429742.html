AT&T Outbids Verizon For Yahoo! - AT&T Inc. (NYSE:T) | Seeking Alpha Verizon (NYSE: VZ ) and AT&T (NYSE: T ) made second-round offers for the Yahoo! (NASDAQ: YHOO ) core internet assets. The company received offers for over $5 billion from AT&T and others, topping the Verizon bid of around $3.5 billion. Your move, Verizon.
