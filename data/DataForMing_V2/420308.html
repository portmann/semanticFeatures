Boeing wins $3.2B precision bomb kit contract - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Jun 1 2016, 05:09 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Boeing (NYSE: BA ) has secured a more than $3B U.S. Air Force contract for Joint Direct Attack Munitions tail kits that use GPS to make conventional bombs into precision-guided weapons. The contract was raised from an initial $1.75B awarded in October 2014, due to warfighter demand and the need to replenish depleted inventories.
