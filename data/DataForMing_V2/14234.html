ETF Hub Mar 26 2015, 17:27 ET | By: Eric Jhonsa , SA News Editor Making Starboard Value happy, Yahoo (NASDAQ: YHOO ) has added $2B to be its buyback authorization, raising its total available funds to $2.73B (good for repurchasing over 6% of outstanding shares at current levels). ( 8-K filing ) The new buyback funds are good until March 31, 2018. Yahoo used part of its Alibaba IPO windfall to buy back $980M worth of shares in Q4, and has spent $9.7B on buybacks since Q2 2012.
