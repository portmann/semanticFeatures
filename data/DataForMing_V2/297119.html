Boeing to cut 747-8 production, take charge in fourth quarter | Reuters Boeing Co (BA.N) said it would lower the production rate on its 747-8 program to match supply with near-term demand in the cargo market. The company said on Thursday it would take a related $569 million, or 84 cents per share, after-tax charge in the fourth quarter of 2015.
