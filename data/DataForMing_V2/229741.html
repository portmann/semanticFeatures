Yahoo hires McKinsey to help with reorganization - Re/code | Reuters Yahoo Inc has appointed management consulting firm McKinsey & Co to help with the reorganization of its core businesses, technology news website Re/code reported on Monday. McKinsey will help Yahoo decide which units to shut, sell or invest in, Re/code said, citing several people close to the situation. ( on.recode.net/1QdGTOu ) Yahoo, which is preparing to spin off its 15 percent stake in Alibaba Group Holding Ltd, and McKinsey were not immediately available for comment.
