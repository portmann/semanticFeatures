BRIEF-Norwegian Air increases Boeing Dreamliner, MAX8 fleets | Reuters * Arctic Aviation Assets Ltd (AAA), a fully owned subsidiary of Norwegian, has signed a letter of agreement with CIT Aerospace for two Boeing 787-9 Dreamliners with delivery in 2018. A fully owned subsidiary of AAA will lease the aircraft * AAA is also exercising eight of its 100 options to purchase Boeing 737 MAX8 * Norwegian currently has eight Boeing 787-8 and two 787-9 in operation, and a total 32 787-9 on order.
