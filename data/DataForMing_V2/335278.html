Ford says Texas truck sales robust despite low oil prices | Reuters A logo of Ford is pictured on a car at the 86th International Motor Show in Geneva, Switzerland, March 1, 2016. DETROIT Ford Motor Co U.S. sales chief Mark LaNeve said on Tuesday that pickup truck sales in Texas have been robust despite the drop in oil prices. Several auto dealership groups have said sales of pickup trucks as well as German luxury car sales in Texas have been pressured by lower energy prices. Ford said it expects February U.S. auto sales industrywide to rise between 9 percent and 10 percent.
