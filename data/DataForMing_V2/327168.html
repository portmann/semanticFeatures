Time Inc, the publisher of Sports Illustrated, People and namesake Time has heard a presentation from Citigroup Inc bankers on pursuing a deal with Yahoo, the Bloomberg report said. ( bloom.bg/1mUM7lQ ) Last week Yahoo officially launched the sale of its core business, which includes search, mail and news sites.
