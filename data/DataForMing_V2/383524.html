Textron wins Boeing 777X flight simulator contract - Textron Inc (NYSE:TXT) | Seeking Alpha Apr 19 2016, 08:13 ET | About: Textron Inc (TXT) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor A unit of Textron (NYSE: TXT ) has won a contract from Boeing to design and manufacture flight simulators for the 777X. The division will produce a suite of equipment to instruct pilots on flying and landing the aircraft in a range of situations, in time for the plane's 2020 delivery date.
