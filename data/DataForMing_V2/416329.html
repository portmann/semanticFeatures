Microsoft, Facebook to jointly build transatlantic subsea cable | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Facebook, Cyclical Consumer Goods May 26 Microsoft Corp and Facebook Inc have agreed to jointly build a subsea cable across the Atlantic Ocean to meet growing demand for high-speed cloud and online services. The construction of the new "MAREA" cable will begin in August and it is expected to be completed in October 2017, the companies said in a statement on Thursday.
