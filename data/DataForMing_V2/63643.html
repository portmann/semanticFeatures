Another Amazon Prime competitor on deck? - eBay Inc. (NASDAQ:EBAY) | Seeking Alpha May 21 2015, 02:58 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor eBay (NASDAQ: EBAY ) is testing an Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) Prime-like program in Germany that it plans to roll out more broadly in the country later this year, although it's still not clear if it would introduce the service elsewhere. The offering, known as eBay+, promises free, fast shipping and returns for customers who, according to local press in Germany, pay €15 to €20 a year.
