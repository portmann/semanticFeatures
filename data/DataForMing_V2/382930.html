YP Holdings plans first-round bid to merge with Yahoo: Bloomberg | Reuters A man walks past a Yahoo logo during the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona, Spain in this February 24, 2016 file photo. YP Holdings LLC, the digital advertising business of what was formerly called Yellowpages.com, is planning to submit a first-round bid on Monday to merge with Yahoo Inc (YHOO.O), Bloomberg reported, citing people familiar with the matter.
