More trouble for daily fantasy sports - The Walt Disney Company (NYSE:DIS) | Seeking Alpha Feb 10 2016, 04:34 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor ESPN (NYSE: DIS ) and DraftKings (Private: DRAFT ) have unwound their exclusive advertising relationship - a deal worth $250M - which will allow ESPN to sell ad time to the site's competitors. Separately, 21st Century Fox ( FOX , FOXA ) has disclosed a write-down on 59% of its investment in DraftKings. During the latter half of 2015, Fox had invested about $160M for a minority stake in the company.
