Report: Boeing to buy directly from new aircraft seat maker - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Apr 5 2016, 08:05 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Boeing (NYSE: BA ) has picked a new aircraft seat supplier for its 737, a move that industry experts said adds competition to leading seat makers Zodiac Aerospace ( OTCPK:ZODFY ) and B/E Aerospace (NASDAQ: BEAV ). In response to questions from Reuters, the planemaker said it will purchase seats directly from the new supplier, LIFT by California-based EnCore.
