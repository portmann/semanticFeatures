Producers are countering the decline in rig count with a drive for efficiency gains in drilling and completion techniques and an increased focus on their more productive acreage.EP Energy is also following the same strategy of focusing on its key assets such as the Eagle Ford and the Wolfcamp in order to optimize production and costs this year , as shown in the chart above . (Ford: Uncertainty) More importantly, the company has also made a smart move by hedging its oil production.In fact, 90% of its production was hedged at the end of the previous quarter.For 2015, EP Energy has hedged 96% of its production at an average price of $91 a barrel.Investors should also note that 95% of EP's hedged production is through fixed price swaps , which means that EP will enjoy strong and predictable cash flows this year despite the weakness in oil pricing. (Uncertainty) Looking ahead, oil prices are expected to make a comeback in the next couple of years as per the EIA .Now, as oil prices move north, EP will be in a good position to enhance its operating and financial performance.The company is capable of producing more oil at lower costs, which is why it enjoys strong margins.In addition, it has made a smart move by hedging almost all of its production at an attractive price.
