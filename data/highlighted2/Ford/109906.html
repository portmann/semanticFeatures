release of Rogue One next year These films will explore a separate part of George Lucas intricate intergalactic universe and will intersperse the new trilogy of Star Wars movies kicking off with Decembers highly anticipated Star Wars The Force Awakens The new Han Solo film will focus on the origin story of the character made famous in the Star Wars films by actor Harrison Ford who will
