no official announcements regarding the companys future production plans The Detroit automakers plans to build more small cars in Mexico are likely to be highly controversial ahead of the November US presidential election Businessman Donald Trump who is seeking to win the nomination to be the Republican presidential candidate has repeatedly blasted US companies including Ford for any moves to
