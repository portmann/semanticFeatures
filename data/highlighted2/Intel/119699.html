If anything , I do n't think that the US government will allow AMD to implode completely , because that would leave Intel as the sole x86 processor producer in the market . That is a bad situation for consumers and for OEMs.With only one supplier, OEM margins will fall, and the costs of this will be partially passed on further to the consumer.For this reason, it is likely that OEMs will be more inclined to award AMD design wins.If the technology is somewhat similar in capability, OEMs will have a greater incentive to give revenue towards AMD to ensure that there remain two suppliers in the market.
