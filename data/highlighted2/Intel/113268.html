With Win10 universal apps running on a unified Intel architecture , the promise of `` cross platform '' compatibility that was never achieved in Win8 gets much closer to reality . There may still be some hiccups in porting a Win10 app from a desktop to a phone, but the process appears to be much easier than with Win8. (Uncertainty) Most importantly, the process also appears to be much easier than what Apple developers face in moving apps between Mac OS X and iOS, which, of course, do run on different hardware platforms.
