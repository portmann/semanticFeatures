Intels SSF platform is actually a Gbsec solution comprising of PCIe adapters processors memory storage switches cables and management software According to Intel the SSF platform offers a port switch which supports more servers at less power consumption compared to an InfiniBand solution of equivalentcost Intels move to acquire InfiniBand from QLogic was aimed at accessing the router technology
