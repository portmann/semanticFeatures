Boeing
Boeing , quite familiar with delivery delays on the Dreamliner and Boeing 747-8 program , has learned its lesson and is not too eager to publicly admit to a ` best case scenario ' service entry target . For Boeing there is more at stake : With major cost overruns on their latest projects the jet maker has to show its ability to deliver a strong product within a specified time frame . (Boeing: Surprise) There is more to win for Boeing by delivering on time , rather than setting an ambitious goal for itself .
