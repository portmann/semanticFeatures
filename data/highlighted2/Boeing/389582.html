Boeing
For the full year Boeing expects margins of around 9 % for its Commercial department . Applying this margin on the estimated revenue the net earnings are expected to be around $1.33bn.Year-over-year this means an 18% decline.To provide a certain earnings range, earnings have also been calculated using a 10% operating margin, in which case the expected earnings are $1.48bn.Boeing 's commercial earnings are likely to see a strong year-over-year decline in Q1 2016 , partly due to lower delivery volume but most of the drop will be caused by lower operating margins . (Boeing: Surprise) This seems to be a trend that has been going on for a while now and is putting pressures on Boeing 's performance .
