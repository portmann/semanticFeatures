Boeings jet would be a blow for Canadas Bombardier which has courted United as a key prospect for its struggling CSeries and potentially Brazils Embraer as well Uniteds requirement for seat aircraft of seat jets it contracts to others to fly under its United Express brand and moving toward larger and more efficient planes flown by its own pilots Uniteds tender for or more seaters ignited a fierce battle at the lower end of the billion annual jet market where the AirbusBoeing duopoly is most under threat Bombardier is looking for a headlinegrabbing win from a big name such as United or JetBlue singleaisle variants as demand surges for more popular seaters But they enjoy significant firepower to offer discounts due to high overall volumes and low marginal costs One consoling factor for
