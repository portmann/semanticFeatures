Boeing
Although one of the biggest customers for Boeing 's wide bodies is currently struggling with its long haul operations , Boeing is leading on the wide body market . Since Airbus discounts its airframes a bit more than Boeing does , Boeing probably will be leading in terms of dollar value by several percentage points . (Boeing: Surprise & Uncertainty) Airbus probably will be leading in terms of units . (Uncertainty) For Boeing to benefit from the rising forecasts and maintain a comfortable market share , it is key for the jet maker to market its Boeing 737MAX for the domestic market and the long haul operations of Indian airlines have to show a more robust growth .
