Boeing X is matching the Airbus Aceo in terms of profit and additionally even has more LD containers available for revenue generating belly freight The Boeing X has more potential than the Airbus Aceo and could even threaten the Airbus Aneo but again thats comparing a comfortable seat aircraft with a less comfortable seat airframe The Airbus A itself is not a bad aircraft The airframe offers superior cabin area which allows for luxury facilities and Gulf carriers are also using this However for other airlines such as Asian and European airlines it is hard to fill the superjumbo The A fits the luxury standard of the countries in the Gulf area but outside of that area the attractiveness of the Airbus A fades rather quickly One of the things that make the Airbus A unattractive is the fact that the design is quite old When the aircraft was being designed it featured revolutionary technology Years later technological advancement leaped leaving the Airbus A as a heavy design with dated engines Especially for airlines that operate the Airbus A with low seat counts the airframe is particularly unattractive as it has low revenue and high per seat costs Not a lot of airlines want to operate a heavy aircraft thats hard to fill when they have the possibility to buy a Boeing X thats more efficient easier to fill and can generate the same profit The Airbus A is too big and has not been sized properly leaving the airframe vulnerable to the Boeing X If Airbus would have analyzed the market a bit better they would have come up with a VLA that carries less passengers and had been sized down for its mission Airbus thought that socalled mega airports would rise for which big aircraft were needed This has not been the case and Airbus did not take proper action to make the Airbus A more attractive Now with the Boeing X coming up Airbus can either come up with the Airbus Aneo or hope for a change in the air travel market that will require onboard luxury facilities The highend market of the Airbus A does not need to fear the Boeing X The Airbus A offers a combination of comfort and capacity that the Boeing X can never achieve The low end market of the Airbus A that seats
