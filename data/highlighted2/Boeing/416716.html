Boeing
In 2015 Boeing achieved its book-to-bill target at the very last moment . Despite lower anticipated deliveries in 2016 , I expect Boeing to be facing a challenge to achieve its book-to-bill target . (Boeing: Surprise & Uncertainty) In April 2015 I published an article in which I said that the order books of jet makers are not bubbles, but should decline at some point.In that article I shared my thoughts and expected order inflow to decline in a few years from now.With oil prices having declined ever since , chances are the order books will decline sooner than I expected at first . (Surprise) With Boeing just barely meeting its target last year , 2016 might be the year in which Boeing will not achieve its book-to-bill ratio .
