Boeing
Take Boeing 's 8,000 job cuts and multiple it across dozens of other multinationals that are facing similar layoffs to react to pricing pressure from European and Japanese competition , and you have the seeds of the next recession . While this recovery has generated plenty of service jobs , the loss of high-quality jobs is much harder to overcome .
