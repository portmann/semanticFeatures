Boeing
In the end , Boeing was not able to seduce enough airlines with discounts and performance improvements . A better move would have been to fill the gap using combi-deals where customers would order the 777X as well as the Boeing 777-300ER . (Boeing: Surprise) Airbus ( OTCPK:EADSF ) proposed such a combi-deal to Delta Air Lines (NYSE: DAL ) in 2014 and it made them win the order eventually.Boeing probably has focused too much on selling the Boeing 777X , while it could have secured some orders for the current Boeing 777 simultaneously .
