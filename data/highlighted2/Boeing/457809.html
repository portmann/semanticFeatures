Boeing
All things considered , the outlook for Boeing 's shares is mixed . On the one hand, its shares are relatively undervalued and investors can expect a dividend that is among the highest of the Dow components.On the other hand , betting on Boeing is betting that the global economy will be in a much better place five years from now . (Boeing: Surprise) We therefore recommend that investors take a cautious approach when investing with Boeing .
