Boeing
Boeing has said it is considering a new jet that would fit a niche between its single-aisle 737 and long-range 787 Dreamliner , and the BofA analysts find `` a strong case '' for Boeing to go ahead , estimating a market for ~ 2,700 planes from airlines to replace older planes or swap in for inefficient , long-range jets that they are currently using for medium-range flights . However , the analysts say Boeing 's ability to recover its cost of capital on the program would depend on executing a more cost effective production method than the 787 , which has struggled with high production costs building the mostly carbon-fiber composite jet .
