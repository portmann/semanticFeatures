(Surprise) Why is this good?Because airlines waste an inordinate amount of time coming and going from gates and waiting for the few tugs they have - especially when banks of flights come into hubs.An airline minute is worth anywhere between $100 to $150.A typical pushback takes up to 12 minutes.With Wheeltug this can be reduced by 40%.Take five minutes per aircraft over six flights per day and the airline has saved up to $4,500 in one day.Now imagine what American ( NASDAQ : AAL ) , Delta ( NYSE : DAL ) , United ( NYSE : UAL ) or Southwest ( NYSE : LUV ) could save over their fleets in a year .
