last obstacles to an historic deal in Vienna that could resolve a more than year dispute over Tehrans nuclear ambitions extending talks through the weekend For Tehran the prospect of sweeping economic sanctions and a longstanding US trade embargo being lifted represents a chance to renew a fleet whose average age of years is almost twice the international average and to do so at affordable prices
