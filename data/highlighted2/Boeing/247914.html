When Indonesia ordered a record number of Boeing 737 aircraft , the CEO was reported as saying he did not have a choice and was pressured by government officials to buy the American aircraft . A similar situation played out when Air China ordered 747-8 aircraft and John Leathy was reported as saying they were unable to sell the A380 to China as it was a political purchase.Vueling airlines of Spain was pressured to buy Airbus aircraft in return for additional landing slots at Paris.So economics , technology and price aside , as China becomes a bigger partner to many countries around the world going forward , many countries will be pressured to buy Chinese aircraft just as many are pressured to buy Boeing and Airbus today .
