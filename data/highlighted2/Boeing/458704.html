Boeing
Boeing started the air show with an 80 units lead over Airbus , but it is very likely that this lead will be completely gone by the end of the air show . Additionally, there is something to be said about this air show in comparison to the previous ones.During previous air shows the share of LoIs (Letter of Intent) and MoUs (Memorandum of Understanding) was relatively high.These order intentions are being firmed up now.This somewhat skews the picture a bit . (Uncertainty) Last year Volga intended to order the Boeing 747-8F and Synergy Group intended to order the Airbus A320neo , those are almost 80 airframes that were booked as order intentions last year but were firmed up during this year 's air show . (Boeing: Uncertainty) What we currently are facing is not a weakness in demand , we are facing the piled up order intentions being firmed up and airlines are waiting for aircraft to show in-fleet performance before committing to more . (Surprise) With one day remaining an important thing to look at is the change of commitment and firm orders year-over-year.Total orders and commitments fell by roughly 40 % . (Uncertainty) Firm orders , so far , are down by roughly 30 % while commitments are down 50 % . (Uncertainty) Current low oil prices give airlines some flexibility to keep existing fleet members in service, which obviously has an impact on options being taken and firmed up.On the other side , with backlogs stretching multiple years into the future the attractiveness of ordering and even taking options fades somewhat .
