With this single order , Boeing will be accounting for 35 % of the total aircraft VietJet has on order . At the same time this order could mean that options for almost 100 Airbus aircraft will not be exercised by VietJet Air . (Uncertainty) For Boeing the order is a win , not only due to size but more due to the fact that it will be getting a share in the fleet of an airlines that used to be an Airbus-only fleet . (Boeing: Surprise) Additionally , VietJet has ambitious expansion plans in a growing market , which could bode well for Boeing .
