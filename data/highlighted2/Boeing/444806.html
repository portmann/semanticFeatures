Boeing
Long-term investors should take a serious look at Boeing , especially after Brexit . The firm's low valuation is not justified, and many research houses seem to agree.S&P and Morningstar maintain buy ratings, with price targets of $160 and $138 respectively.Last week Morgan Stanley initiated coverage on BA with an "overweight" rating, owing to recent stock price underperformance and margin upside potential.The weak military spending environment in the US is one area of concern, but increased demand from foreign customers is helping to pick up the slack.And , if Trump wins in November , things could really get interesting for Boeing .
