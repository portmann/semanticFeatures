Boeing
I think that Boeing did a fine job in communicating with its investors when it announced it would be taking billions in charges . Some of these charges were already expected by analysts , which means that there were no big surprises when the charge was announced nor in its earnings report . (Surprise) Additionally, the underlying results exceeded expectations.At the time of writing , shares are trading 1.8 % higher , quite impressive given the fact that the company just showed the impact of a multi-billion charge on its earnings .
