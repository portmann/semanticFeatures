Boeing 777-10X : The Jumbo Killer - The Boeing Company ( NYSE : BA ) | Seeking Alpha Boeing 777-10X will , as expected , compete with the A380 on the lower end of the 400-500 seat market . (Surprise) Boeing 777-10X will be the final nail in the coffin of the Boeing 747-8I.Sources state Boeing ( NYSE : BA ) has been proposing a possible Boeing 777-10X jet to their customers . (Uncertainty) In this article, I will have a look at how this will affect the competitive playing field and how this places the Boeing 777X in the market.Just like the Boeing 777X family , the Boeing 777-10X is a push towards bigger aircraft but not too big . (Surprise) The Boeing 777-10X jet would turn the 2-member Boeing 777X family into a 3-member family , which could potentially make all members of the 777X family more attractive for airlines . (Surprise & Uncertainty) Table 1 : Seating and range for the Boeing 777X The Boeing 777-10X would have 25 seats ( 4 rows ) more than the Boeing 777-9 , which has 50 seats more than the -8 . (Surprise) The range of the Boeing 777-10X is still unknown and probably one of the main subjects of discussions with airlines . (Uncertainty) The range requirement is one of the most important factors that could determine the success of the airplane . (Uncertainty) Assuming that the propulsion system and the wing planform stay the same , an educated guess for the range is 7000 nm - 7200 nm . (Uncertainty) AeroAnalysis expects that customers will require the airframe to have a range of 7500 nm , hence more fuel tanks , bigger engines or a bigger planform might be required . (Surprise & Uncertainty) On paper, the Boeing 777-10X does not have any competitors.The Boeing 777-10X is seen by many as a direct competitive threat to the Airbus ( OTCPK:EADSF ) A380.The Airbus A380 can seat up to 544 passengers with a range capability of 8200 nm.Those are the pretty figures on paper.However , there are a lot of Airbus A380s flying around with less than 500 seats . (Surprise) The Boeing 777-10X could effectively compete with the Airbus A380 and even beat the airframe in terms of cost efficiency . (Uncertainty) What needs to be said is that the Airbus A380 will be superior in the 500+ seat market, but this market is a small one and likely unprofitable for any player that wants to be active in this niche.Also for airlines , the 500 + seat segment is a risky one . (Uncertainty) The Airbus A380 has high capital costs and has shown to be hard to fill, making it very cost inefficient to operate.On the other hand, the Airbus A380 offers a standard of luxury that even a Boeing 777-10X cannot offer.So the Boeing 777-10X will target the lower bracket of the Airbus A380 market , which can not be operated with profits . (Surprise) Although the Boeing 777-10X can compete with the Airbus A380, I think Boeing will provide the ultimate blow to their very own Boeing 747-8I when they launch the Boeing 777-10X.The Boeing 777-9 can be operated in 3-class configuration with approximately 357 seats , while the Boeing 747-8I has 8 more revenue generating seats . (Surprise & Uncertainty) The Boeing 777-10X would have around 378-405 seats in 3-class configuration - thirteen more than the Boeing 747-8I . (Surprise) This means that the Boeing 747-8I will be squeezed by the -9 and the -10 X. I think one of the most important conclusions that can be drawn is that the Boeing 777-10X would serve 2 purposes : Compete with Airbus A380 on the lower end of the 400-500 seat market . (Surprise) Replace the Boeing 747-8I.With the Boeing 777-9 , Boeing already has a replacement for the Boeing 747-8I but it could be lacking some space to put some luxury in place . (Uncertainty) With the Boeing 777-10X , Boeing can either offer that extra space to add luxury and comfort or offer higher revenue potential by adding additional seats . (Surprise) An important thing to note is that the seat delta (difference in number of seats) between a Boeing 777-10X and Airbus A380 in 3-class configuration is substantial.There are only a few airlines that operate the Airbus A380 with approximately 405 seats . (Uncertainty) Over the past few days I have read and heard many people stating that the Boeing 777-10X is a hypothetical aircraft for an unsuccessful market segment.I think the Boeing 777-10X does make sense in some way, but will not be able to beat the Airbus A380 in terms of luxury.The Boeing 777-10X would be geared towards the requirements set by the Gulf carriers; carriers that currently are operating the Airbus A380, even on the unsuccessful routes.The reason for the market segment being ` unsuccessful ' is because the Airbus A380 in fact is too big to operate with less than 450 seats . (Surprise) So far airlines have been serving this segment with an oversized airplane.The Boeing 777-10X seems to be a better fit for the job and Boeing already knows who will be buying it . (Surprise & Uncertainty) It will take a while before the Boeing 777-10X will be launched, but what bodes well is that the Boeing 777X is a development that is supported by customer feedback, whereas the Airbus A380 was a prestige project that did and still does not center on customer feedback.In the end , the Boeing 777-10X could be the nail in the coffin of superjumbos in general , not only the Boeing 747-8I or Airbus A380 .
