to emulate Both Google and Microsoft have amassed huge bases of users for their operating systems based on the commodity PC model Microsoft still has a user base for Windows it claims is about billion strong Likewise Google claims to have billion Android users Under the circumstances one has to wonder why Apples competitors field their own branded mobile devices The answer of course is smartphone industry profits Apple generally charges a premium for all its devices and its mobile devices have become its main revenue source Apples revenue and profit now dwarf that of its competitors despite having fewer users for iOS and Mac OS X The device events of Apple Microsoft and Google have served to underscore that theres a missing element in Microsoft and Google devices that is present in Apples mobile devices This is the custom system on chip SOC that powers all iPhones iPads Apple TVs and iPods Touch The lack of ability to design their own custom SOCs relegates the smartphones of
