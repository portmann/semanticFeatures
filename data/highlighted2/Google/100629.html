While Google 's ads encourage link clicks ( Facebook 's encourage likes ) that lead to a higher chance of product purchases , it does n't give advertisers the ability to sale from the ad itself . Facebook will be the first to do so, and that adds value.Therefore , given the disconnect between Facebook and Google 's ad prices , combined with Facebook having the best platform to provide direct selling due to user and consumer engagement , my belief is that ad prices for Facebook are going to soar quickly , and consequently , Google 's could decline faster .
