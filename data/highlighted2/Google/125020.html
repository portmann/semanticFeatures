However, I want investors to remember some of these key positives for Apple: The iPhone had the highest quarterly switching rate from Android that Apple has ever measured.Only 27% of Apple's installed base has upgraded to the 6 or 6 Plus so far.Phone ASP rose sequentially, the opposite of the $25 average sequential decline seen in the last four years from Q2 to Q3.The iPad sold nearly 11 million units, and Apple reportedly has 76% of the market for over $200 tablets.
