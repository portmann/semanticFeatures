For consumers there are certain areas of the market where competition is clear cut When it comes to soda for example the products are on shelves next to each other and competition is clearly defined When it comes to Netflix NASDAQ NFLX however I often see a lack of understanding when it comes to this key business item In this article I explore the issue a little further as it goes to the heart of news about someone else making a play in this space Recently Rogers Communications NYSE RCI and Shaw Communications NYSE SJR have announced that they are opening their streaming video service Shomi to all Canadians Previously the service was only allowed to the companies subscribers It is clear from the comment section of that news piece and Ive seen this plenty of times regarding streaming key competitive aspects many lose track of is that this adds another content bidder to the space especially if the Shomi service generates a fair amount of revenues Lets say hypothetically that Im
