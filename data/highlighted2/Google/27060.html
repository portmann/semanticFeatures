against it but the company is increasingly self satisfied While the SP is up over the last year Google NASDAQ GOOG NASDAQ GOOGL shares have advanced just This is not a mirage and its not a shortterm trend either Google is becoming what Microsoft NASDAQ MSFT became in the late s slow bureaucratic timid and way too selfsatisfied for its own good The problem for Microsoft started with the Clinton Administration which launched an antitrust case against it in While the government lost its case it saddled the company with a host of lawyers analysts and public relations people who found it was easier to say no to new ideas and milk the WindowsOffice monopoly than to try something new While the Obama Administration looks more kindly toward Google than the Clintons did Microsoft this is not true in most of the world The European Union is planning formal antitrust charges Russia and China have kept out Google with their own versions Yandex NASDAQ YNDX and Baidu NASDAQ BIDU and other governments around the world routinely seek to censor it in various ways Google is a onetrick pony built on Internet advertising and the world is not going along with the trick anymore Worse is the selfsatisfaction coming out of the Googleplex so reminiscent of when Microsoft ruled and everyone who was supersmart wanted to work there Bragging about your job interview process or about how most employees are paid like they are stupid is arrogance personified Bragging about the poor performance of your stock is arrogance to the nth degree The fact is that Googles growth continues to slow and its margins continue to shrink The deterioration has been slow revenue growth falling from to over three years and net income falling from over of revenue three years ago to last year Just as Microsoft tried many things to get out of its Windows dependence and eventually failed so Google is proving unable to find new profit centers Android is dominant but not profitable The same is true for Chromecast and Chromebooks Google Wallet has been trumped by Apple Pay and threats to everyone in the space is not the same thing as taking share If most CEOs were seeing deteriorating results selfsatisfaction at every hand and an inability to break out of the original niche theyd be worried and activists might be pounding on the door A dualshare structure assures this cannot happen at Google Its good to be the king but only for the king What all this tells me is that investors have been right in dismissing the stock and that the ennui is set to continue indefinitely into the future
