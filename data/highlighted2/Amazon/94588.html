Amazon tinkers e-book author pay structure - Amazon.com , Inc. ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) | Seeking Alpha Jun 23 2015 , 04:37 ET | By : Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Starting next month , Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) will overhaul the way it pays royalties to self-published authors on its e-book platform , by rewarding them based on the number of pages read , rather than the number of times their book has been borrowed .
