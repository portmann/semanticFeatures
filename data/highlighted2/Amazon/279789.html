(Uncertainty) Depending on the actions of various uncontrollable subatomic particles , the cat would live or be killed . (Uncertainty) If the radioactive particles decayed fast enough , the radioactivity would discharge and kill the cat . (Uncertainty) If not , the cat would live . (Surprise & Uncertainty) Or the bears may be right that the company is not profitable and that selling on cost is n't a winning strategy .
