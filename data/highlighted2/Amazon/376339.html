To Amazon 's credit , they recognize the platform potential of Alexa and are actively encouraging other device makers to use Alexa in their own hardware designs and to create add-on skills . This could allow Amazon to do an end-run around the existing ecosystem players , such as Apple ( NASDAQ : AAPL ) , Google ( NASDAQ : GOOG ) ( NASDAQ : GOOGL ) and Microsoft ( NASDAQ : MSFT ) , and place it at the forefront of voice-based computing .
