Amazon
In my opinion , Amazon is growing into its shoes , provided it continues to execute on its growth initiatives and improving its margins . Is there a significant gamble taking place in going long?Certainly.We assumed top line growth growing at 18.6 % annually on average for the next five years . (Uncertainty) That's a massive number for a company with sales already in excess of $100B.We also assumed nearly 20 % in steady annual operating margin improvement - while the starting point is low , that is a fair challenge for even the best management . (Uncertainty) Given the lack of forward guidance present from management, each individual investor here has to set their own expectations and be willing to trade around those expectations when they are either not met or exceeded.Overall , the path to a fairer valuation is there , and I can understand Amazon bulls ' fervor , assuming the company can execute .
