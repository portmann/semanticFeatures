I own Amazon.Com ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) and I 'm not selling the shares in my retirement account . In fact , I am very likely to pick up a few more in a few weeks . (Surprise) Because Amazon is about to be hit by a wave of selling pressure . There is a lot of bearish sentiment around.When Goldman Sachs (NYSE: GS ) goes down, stocks go down.The Wal-Mart (NYSE: WMT ) debacle has extended to other big box names, like Target (NYSE: TGT ), Kroger (NYSE: KR ) and Dollar Tree (NASDAQ: DLTR ), all of whom were hit yesterday.Netflix (NASDAQ: NFLX ), another high-flying tech name, got hit after an earnings miss.More important , I think that expectations for Amazon , which reports earnings next Thursday , have been set too high . (Amazon: Surprise) The official "whisper number" is for a loss of 7 cents per share on revenues of $24.91 billion.But when you look at a market cap of $ 256 billion for a company that is mainly engaged in retail , and admit that 's at least 2.5 times sales , the real `` whisper-upon-whisper '' number is probably much , much higher . (Surprise & Uncertainty) I think anything short of $ 25 billion in sales , and an actual profit , will prove to be a disappointment . (Surprise) For its second quarter , for instance , the company reported that cloud revenue through Amazon Web Services was $ 1.8 billion , against $ 1 billion a year earlier , 80 % growth year-over-year . (Amazon: Surprise) If AWS revenues do n't grow 80 % year-over-year for the third quarter , that will be a selling excuse . (Uncertainty) Or , analysts could dig into the profitability of Amazon Prime Video , which competes with Netflix . (Amazon: Uncertainty) What are its expenses?What portion of that $99/year charge is going to video, as opposed to subsidizing shipping?How much is it paying to license content from CBS (NYSE: CBS ) and other studios, which are also focused on building up rival Hulu .The `` oh-noes '' could even hit through Wal-Mart , which said it would be investing in technology to compete with Amazon e-tailing over the next two years . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Wo n't that hit Amazon 's top-line and bottom-line ? Maybe so . (Uncertainty) Sell, sell, sell.All these are really just excuses, as if people need one in the current market.Amazon Web Services is killing other clouds and disrupting the entire technology marketplace . Amazon is not just `` giving away '' content through Prime - it 's selling and renting it too . Amazon is working hard to disrupt the delivery space as well - taking on partners like Federal Express ( NYSE : FDX ) and UPS ( NYSE : UPS ) with stores and drones and even customers delivering packages to other customers . The fact is that when the bear market knives come out, they come out for everyone.Excuses are made, before or after the sell orders come in.The August lows have to be tested , and retested , before anyone is going to believe that we 're heading higher . (Surprise & Uncertainty) In that environment , I would not be surprised to see Amazon trade below $ 500 again by the end of this month .
