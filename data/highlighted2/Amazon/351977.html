Very casual gamers and more well off gamers where an extra $5-$20 isn't worth a 30 or 45 minute trip to a GameStop store.Of course Microsoft denied via Twitter that digital trade-ins are being considered.But there is a big difference between not being considered now and being ruled out for eternity .
