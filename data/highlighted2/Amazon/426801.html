Amazon prepares mega investment in India - Amazon.com , Inc. ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) | Seeking Alpha Jun 8 2016 , 03:34 ET | By : Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) is planning to invest an additional $ 3B in India , boosting its committed investment in the country to over $ 5B . The company has so far built 21 fulfillment centers across the nation , and expects to open a Web Services Cloud Region and the firm 's largest software development center outside of the U.S. Overall , Amazon 's international segment ( all markets outside North America ) is growing much more slowly than its home base , with sales rising 5.7 % in 2015 to $ 35.4 B vs. 25 % in North America to $ 63.7 B.
