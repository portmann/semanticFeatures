Actually, the news from overnight wasn't just that an investigation was conducted, but rather that offices were "raided".Reuters from Tokyo reported , Japan 's Fair Trade Commission has raided the offices of Amazon.com Inc 's local unit on suspicion of pressuring retailers to offer products at lower prices than on rival sites , the Nikkei business daily said on Monday .
