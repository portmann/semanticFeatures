Amazon
As companies like Apple , Google and Facebook struggle to penetrate the e-commerce and online transactions markets , Amazon has a vast payment portfolio that includes services for in-store transactions , online transactions , and checkout solutions . Amazon can do much more than it does to generate additional revenues from the payment business that is relatively easy for a company with such an impressive payment portfolio , alongside with its endeavors in the shipment drones and hardware area .
