Amazon : The $ 50 Fire Tablet Is A Ruthless Killer Idea - Amazon.com , Inc. ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) | Seeking Alpha Amazon is no longer among the top five global vendors of tablets . Amazon is now accepting pre-orders of its $ 49.99 7-inch Fire Tablet . The last time Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) was among the world 's top vendor of tablets was in Q4 2014 . The Fire tablet has seen a big drop in sales over the first half of this year.Amazon dropped out of the top 5 rankings of the biggest tablet vendors this first half of 2015 . Amazon 's declining fortune on tablet sales may soon reverse course . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Amazon 's new $ 49.99 Fire Android tablet is now available for pre-order and it will ship out on September 30 . Selling a $ 50 tablet is a ruthless strategy that could eventually kill sales of other Android tablet vendors . (Uncertainty) Even Samsung ( OTC : SSNLF ) , the world 's top vendor of Android tablets , will find it pyrrhic to compete with a $ 50 Amazon Fire tablet . Unless LG Electronics ( OTC : LGEAF ) , Lenovo ( OTCPK : LNVGY ) , and Huawei match the cutthroat price slice to $ 50 price tag , they too will suffer from Amazon 's merciless pricing strategy . I do not doubt that Amazon is selling its new Fire tablet below cost . (Amazon: Uncertainty) My speculation that it is being sold at a loss is helped by the fact that this $ 50 Android tablet could run the advanced 3D mobile game Real Racing 3 . (Uncertainty) The rather stiff hardware requirements of Real Racing 3 means Amazon is using a good quality ( not cheap ) quad-core processor with advanced GPU . Amazon is also hyping up its $ 49.99 Fire Tablet , claiming it has a more durable build quality than the iPad Air 2 from Apple ( NASDAQ : AAPL ) . (Amazon: Surprise) Amazon expects to recoup its gamble that some buyers of the $ 49.99 Fire tablet would become repeat buyers of Amazon movies and other goods . This is the greatest advantage of Amazon over its tablet competitors . It can afford to sell hardware at a loss and still come out a winner.The people who buy the $ 50 Fire tablet will no doubt get inundated with enticing ads to purchase something from Amazon stores . (Amazon: Uncertainty) The custom Fire OS 5 Android operating system is just another channel to attract more people to shop on Amazon . (Amazon: Surprise) Unlike before , Samsung is no longer selling its entry-level Android tablets on Amazon . (Amazon: Surprise) A $50 Fire tablet simply kills the competition by its price alone.Samsung sells its cheapest 7-inch tablet, the Galaxy Tab 3 Lite at $129 .The Tab 3 Lite also has 1GB of RAM and 8GB of ROM storage but it only has a dual-core processor.This still makes it less powerful than the much cheaper $ 49.99 Fire tablet . (Surprise) Samsung and other tablet makers can not offer access to the same 38 million movies and TV shows that Amazon devices now enjoy . The Android App Store of Samsung is also smaller than the 300,000 apps available from the Amazon App Store . (Amazon: Surprise) What makes the $ 49.99 Fire tablet an impulse smart buy for me is the new Amazon Underground app store . It offers people the chance to get free paid Android apps/games, and also free in-app purchases on freemium games.While Google ( NASDAQ : GOOGL ) Play Store sells Goat Simulator for 190 pesos ( $ 3.99 ) , Amazon Underground offers it free to Fire Tablet owners . Such generosity from Amazon puts other Android tablet vendors in a no-win situation . This is especially true in markets where there are operating Amazon stores like North America , Europe , India , and China . My point is that Samsung , Lenovo , and Huawei could not afford to match the subsidized hardware and apps that a $ 49.99 Amazon Fire tablet is now offering . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Even Apple , with its $ 200 billion cash hoard , will not dare match the free paid apps offer of Amazon Underground just to make its $ 399 iPad Mini 4 more attractive . (Amazon: Surprise) Amazon is also generous enough to offer free unlimited cloud storage for photos and videos recorded from a Fire tablet . Samsung does not offer such unlimited cloud storage feature to buyers of its entry-level tablets.For in-device storage expansion, the Fire tablet's microSD slot supports up to 128GB cards.The Galaxy Tab 3 Lite only supports up to 32GB microSD cards.The battery life of the new Fire tablet is also decent at 7 hours when web surfing/watching videos.Jeff Bezos thinks like no other capitalists on the planet.Where other people/investors see a losing proposition, Bezos sees golden opportunity.The $ 49.99 Fire tablet is another example of Bezos ' unique approach to building up Amazon 's future . Bezos is patently merciless when it comes to ensuring that more people will make Amazon their official online shopping site . (Amazon: Surprise) He does n't feel the pain of selling tablets at zero margins because he believes their buyers will eventually become addicted to making repeat purchases from Amazon . (Amazon: Uncertainty) I heartily agree with Bezos' thinking.I therefore endorse a Hold rating for AMZN.On the other hand a Buy is also reasonable ... many Bezos believers are still making the decades-old Amazon perform like a momo growth stock .
