AWS already produces 40 % of Amazon 's operating profit by segment . (Surprise & Uncertainty) Over the years , I have watched , and been an Amazon customer , as the company increased the scope of its retail business and radically changed the way most people in this country think about online shopping . (Surprise) AWS revenues are currently growing at about 70 % year on year which is more or less unprecedented for an operation that is approaching a $ 10 billion/year run rate . (Surprise) These days , people are trying to determine if that kind of growth might continue and if so for how long and at what level of profitability .
