itself Its investments are hurting current earnings but the payoff will bring higher earnings One thing about investors I never understand is their disdain for a company investing in itself They for the upfront costs involved However if you have the wherewithal to look beyond the current expenses you will likely be handsomely rewarded when these investments pay off For such a steady and slowgrowth company thats a lot of volatility So why the big drop It has to do with a few things Higherthanexpected shrink a quarter ago a feeling that Amazon NASDAQ AMZN and online retailers are and started instituting price hikes I remember it well because I thought it was a big mistake I was a fan of the previous head of US Operations Eduardo CastroWright who wanted to push WalMarts what can we expect from the new CEO of US operations Greg Foran Low prices thank God This guy gets it seemingly that value is the raison detre for the company This is one of my keys to value investing WalMarts infamously low wages agreeing first to raise workers wages to then dollars an hour I think this is an outstanding idea for several reasons Lets turn to another chart Costco NASDAQ COST
