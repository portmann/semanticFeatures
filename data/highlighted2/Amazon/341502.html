Open source software like Linux and Apache Web Server have disrupted the Commercial Server business to a degree, but many startups still use Microsoft technologies and large businesses regard the large, experienced army of Microsoft coders as mission critical.The software is known intimately by its user base.Switching to new operating systems and software applications is not just too disruptive for consumers, but even more so for companies who have built up their business processes for many years and decades upon Microsoft technology.My conclusion is that about 60% of Microsoft revenues come with significant pricing power.3.Are the revenues or gross profits of the business tied to fickle commodity prices?Again, 60% of Microsoft revenues are based upon the software it has developed for decades.The revenue and costs streams are not tied to highly volatile forces like commodity prices .
