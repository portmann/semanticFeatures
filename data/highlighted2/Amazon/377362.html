Apple Needs One More Big Thing - Apple Inc. ( NASDAQ : AAPL ) | Seeking Alpha Apple is priced like a fading industrial company . (Surprise) It needs something big to grow younger again . (Surprise) Apple (NASDAQ: AAPL ) grew up under Tim Cook.It did all the things Wall Street wanted - it bought back stock, it split, it paid a nice dividend - and it is paying the price for that.(The lesson is that tech companies should The current Apple price/earnings multiple of 11 is like that of a fading industrial company.It 's closer to that of IBM ( NYSE : IBM ) than it is Microsoft ( NASDAQ : MSFT ) - a lot closer . (Surprise) The assumption is that next Monday , it will just announce a smaller iPhone , maybe new Apple Watch software and that it faces some major capital requirements to build out iCloud . (Surprise & Uncertainty) If Apple is to grow younger again , if it 's going to get that P/E even halfway to Microsoft 's ( and double the value of the stock in the process ) it needs to wow us . (Surprise) It needs to create, from whole cloth, a niche as powerful as the iPod, iPad and iPhone were "back in the day," which is to say the last decade.Thus, the Apple Car.Cook knows how the hype game is played here.The anticipation matters more than what is delivered . (Surprise & Uncertainty) The car could include a drive train , a new engine technology , and a new way of selling . (Uncertainty) Or it could be a component in someone else 's vehicle . (Uncertainty) Cook won't say because Cook doesn't know.He will tease it , just as Microsoft teased Windows in the 1980s , hoping the Fear , Uncertainty and Doubt this creates will slow other players and draw the right answer to him . (Uncertainty) Buying Netflix ( NASDAQ : NFLX ) , as some have suggested , will not move the needle because Netflix is ferociously expensive - its P/E rivals that of Amazon.Com ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) - and because it would require major investment to do what the speculators really want , build out a global cloud infrastructure that would let it take Amazon on . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Apple could announce a new version of Apple TV , and the FCC 's action on set-top boxes could give the new product a big boost , but if that were to be in the offing a play for Viacom ( NASDAQ : VIA ) ( NASDAQ : VIAB ) , CBS ( NYSE : CBS ) or even Sony ( NYSE : SNE ) Entertainment would be far more likely than one for Netflix . (Surprise & Uncertainty) Sony would not only bring Apple movies and shows for Apple TV, but Michael Jackson's copyrights , which include those of the Beatles.This would bring the Apple story full-circle , since the corporate moniker name was a tribute to the Beatles ' Apple Records label , and the group has gotten wealthier off that than off their own songs .
