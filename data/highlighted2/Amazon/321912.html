ways to provide customers with faster service and delivery partners with more opportunities In a recent earnings call an Amazon executive said the company was not trying to replace partner carriers but rather find ways to handle more of its own deliveries at peak times Amazon Flex works like ondemand ridehailing service Uber Drivers use the app to sign up for shifts to pick up packages at small
