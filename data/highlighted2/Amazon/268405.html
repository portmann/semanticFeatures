Cargo Facts reports Amazon is `` negotiating for the acquisition of perhaps 20 '' 767s . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Amazon , which has been running an air cargo trial out of Wilmington , Ohio , is reportedly looking to avoid delays at the hands of UPS , FedEx , and other logistics partners - two years ago , UPS ' inability to keep up with Amazon 's holiday orders led many Christmas gifts to arrive after Dec. 25 . One cargo industry source says Amazon `` expects to make a decision to go beyond the trial run and pull the trigger on a larger air cargo operation by the end of January .
