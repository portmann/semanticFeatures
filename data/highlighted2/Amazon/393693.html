Amazon
Amazon is the most concerning allocation in the top holdings , but I recognize that it is growing market share very effectively by operating without a profit ( or tiny profits ) while huge economies of scale are established . By the time it raises prices a few points to create earnings , I may not even remember that there are traditional stores that sell some of the things I purchase .
