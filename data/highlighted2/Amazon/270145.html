an ecommerce platform that title belongs to eBay NASDAQ EBAY In fact BABA is building an ecosystem consisting of media cloud payment and OO that will ultimately drive the growth of its core business In the case with media investors can think of BABAs media deals similar to that of Amazon NASDAQ AMZN Prime Video see Amazon Scaling Up Online Video and Amazon Prime Ecosystem Expansion where media is another valueadd service that BABA can add as part of its ecommerce bundle similar to that of Amazon Prime In my view BABA has the best assets among its Chinese ecommerce peers and is the only company BABAs deal with Disney NYSE DIS involves an OTT product called DisneyLife through which the user can access DIS content and plan their trips to the Hong Kong and upcoming Shanghai Disney parks DisneyLife is featured on BABAs TMall site priced at a bit pricey in my view given how Xiaomi or LeTV settop box cost half the price and will begin shipping on December th Besides selling the OTT product on TMall the partnership also includes content distribution given that DIS cannot operate its own channel in China BABAs presence spans across settop boxes and smart TV partnerships that can allow users to easily access DIS content and this will be critical for DIS to build a longterm viewer base in China I note that DIS new Star Wars The Force Awakens movie will likely see some challenges when it hits the Chinese theaters next year as most Chinese viewers are not familiar with the Star Wars franchise and do not understand its significance That said DIS needs BABAs partnership to build its viewership in China that could potentially be the single most important market for DIS in the next decade The other media deal involved Bona Films NASDAQ BONA which will be was screened across North America earlier this year In essence securing key media assets will differentiate BABAs ecosystem versus that of its rivals and this is a key reason behind BABAs recent moves
