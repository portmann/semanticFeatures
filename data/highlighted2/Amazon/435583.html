(Uncertainty) Twitter will let advertisers target users based on emojis - While Twitter (NYSE: TWTR ) has been facing ad execution issues for a while, the company remains willing to think outside the box as it rolls out one ad product after another.Its latest offering lets marketers target users who have written or engaged with tweets featuring emojis - one example given is a pizza shop targeting a user who tweeted a pizza emoji.The option follows the launch of an ad format that can (with a user's consent) leverage user tweets .It has a bit in common with efforts by Facebook advertisers to use Facebook 's new Reactions buttons/emojis to get a better read on how users feel about ads and other content , and to deliver ads accordingly . (Surprise) But in many cases , Twitter 's solution will probably be less precise in gauging sentiment .
