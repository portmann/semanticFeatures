The set will be $120, extra action figures are $14.99 and the special Prowler Bot is $39.99.That'll set you back as much as $175, so you can understand why the demos are important to educate the parents about why they should buy them.Disney has a long line-up of movies coming out over the next 2-3 years and developing these kinds of toys and consumer goods allows the company to push sales beyond the regular action figures and games.This highlights the reason why Disney decided to merge its consumer products and interactive segments, which it announced at the end of June.With wearable tech and apps, the company can expand the scope and playability of its toys and goods.In the company's Q2 2015, these two segments accounted for $1.2 billion, or about 10%, of revenues and $388 million in operating income.Editor-in-chief and CEO Jim Silver of toy review site TTPM said he projects the Avengers line of connected toys could be a $ 500 million a year business , but could expand to a $ 1 billion annual business once the Star Wars and Frozen sets are out on the market .
