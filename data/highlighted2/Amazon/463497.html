Amazon
I see this stock trading at a premium today , but I also see huge growth on the horizon for Amazon , which can justify the small premium to many investors . Investors looking for a stock over a 5 or 10-year span will probably find Amazon as an attractive investment , as I believe this is a long-term winner . (Amazon: Uncertainty) However , if investors are looking at a 12-month timeline , I would wait for a correction on this one .
