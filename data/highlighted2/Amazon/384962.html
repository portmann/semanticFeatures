Amazon
In order to justify its current valuation , at a standard tech price/earnings multiple of 30 , Amazon would need to report $ 5.29 / share in net income this quarter ( $ 21 per year multiplied by 30 is $ 630 ) , with visibility on those profits going forward . That may happen someday , but it will only happen at the cost of the capital budget , which for a utility is the one thing that you ca n't cut , lest you wither and begin to die .
