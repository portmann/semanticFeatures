(Uncertainty) This is an interesting development.Back in September , I suggested that Disney had an ace up its sleeve , because of its ability to offer a skinny bundle via Hulu , given its ABC , ESPN , A&E and Disney content . (Uncertainty) In particular , Adult Swim is apparently quite popular with Millennials , which `` attracts 60 % more 18 - to 34-year-olds than its nearest competitor , 60 % more , '' Bewkes said .
