When Amazon ships product across the continent the parcel will go through at least six sorts when shipped via a PL network By utilizing its own network the product would only be sorted three times Amazon however is not building a MANYtoMANY network It is building a ONEtoMANY network PLs ship PARCELS Amazon ships PRODUCT This distinction is important as it removes a lot of complexity from the shipping process and optimizes the network for BULK PRODUCT SHIPPING Amazons network of Fulfillment Centers brings packers into the equation an asset the PLs would be hard pressed to replicate without completely rebuilding the networks from the ground up Fulfillment Centers turn PRODUCT into PARCELs at the last step prior to delivery It is very easy to take a pallet of goods at a central hub and Amazon Flex It should be noted that the trinkets shipped from the Central Hub were not sorted en route to the Fulfillment Center at a Regional Air Hub This allows Amazons Regional Air hubs to be
