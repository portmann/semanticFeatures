How successful will Amazon be ? Judging by Xiaomi 's projected 100m handset shipment for this year selling high-end smartphones that are priced at $ 350/unit , I would argue that Amazon could see some success . (Amazon: Uncertainty) The downside will be ongoing losses attributed to the hardware strategy due to an increase in R&D and marketing costs.However , looking in the long term , it may just be the route Amazon should take in order to justify its hardware strategy .
