Netflix does not produce a compelling product or profits like Amazon , Facebook ( NASDAQ : FB ) , or Google , and over time will not continue to trade at comparable pie-in-the-sky valuations . It would take quite a compelling bull thesis to suggest that NFLX has any positive catalyst due in the future . (Uncertainty) Rave reviews of future content will not be enough to move the stock significantly , and only a remarkable Q2/Q3 earnings beat could get the stock moving again , but more than likely only run-ups into earnings can be expected from here with disappointing price action to follow . (Surprise & Uncertainty) The market could hit new highs this summer , but that does not guarantee NFLX participates , especially given the amount of sector rotation the market has experienced this year .
