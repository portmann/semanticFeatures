Before the skeptics downplay the feasibility of unbundling , we can look no further than Amazon 's recent decision to unbundle its Prime services and Prime Video . AMZN's decision to unbundle the Prime annual service is a clear move to drive the adoption of its two-day unlimited free shipping service.As for Prime Video , the platform has certainly improved significantly over the past years and some could argue may be comparable to Netflix ( NASDAQ : NFLX ) .
