Amazon gets into private-label foods - Amazon.com , Inc. ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) | Seeking Alpha Jun 29 2016 , 15:38 ET | By : Stephen Alpher , SA News Editor Beginning this week , Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) quietly began offering for sale whole-bean and ground coffee under its new Happy Belly label - putting it in competition with the likes of Starbucks ( NASDAQ : SBUX ) . The WSJ one month ago reported Amazon as planning four brands of private-label foods , diapers , and cleaning supplies to be sold exclusively to Amazon Prime members . For the moment , Amazon is not necessarily positioning these items as the low-cost offering . Bags of Happy Belly cost $ 9.99 each - about $ 2 more than Starbucks ' breakfast blend , but $ 3 less than Starbucks ' French roast .
