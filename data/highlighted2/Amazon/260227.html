(Uncertainty) There was an original scare some months back , when an IRS executive made comments at a conference that seemed to indicate that companies spinning off other companies for tax purposes would soon be coming to an end . (Uncertainty) Of course, everybody thought this had something to do with Yahoo and the stock sold off on that Friday before the close.Yahoo 's CEO and Board of Directors subsequently made statements that they did not believe there was going to be any issues moving forward with the spin off as planned .
