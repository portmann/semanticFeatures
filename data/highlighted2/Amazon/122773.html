Amazon : Price Ecosystem Expanding , AWS Accelerating - Amazon.com , Inc. ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) | Seeking Alpha AMZN will report on the 23rd with consensus expecting - $ 0.14 in EPS and $ 22.37 b in revenue . Prime ecosystem could gain further traction , a negative to eBay . (Uncertainty) Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) will report its quarter on the 23rd . Consensus expects a loss of $0.14 and $22.37b in revenue.Heading in the quarter, I will be focusing on the status of the Prime ecosystem and performance of the AWS segment.Judging by the solid performance we saw from both segments last quarter, I would remain bullish on AMZN shares.For Prime , the ecosystem is gaining traction in North America and I believe that the higher penetration over time will make AMZN a stickier ecommerce platform , which will be a negative for rival eBay ( NASDAQ : EBAY ) . (Surprise & Uncertainty) One interesting data point is the recent Prime Day when we saw sales on that single day surpassing that of Black Friday.It is quite impressive given that Prime Day was limited to AMZN Prime users rather than all the consumers.The idea of Prime shoppers generating sales that was more than that generated by both Prime and non-Prime shoppers on Black Friday is simply mind boggling and it really highlights the stickiness and the adoption of the Prime ecosystem . (Surprise) The combination of cloud service , online video and two-day shipping already offer the consumers significant value-add , and I believe that AMZN will continue to expand Prime services similar to that of Apple ( NASDAQ : AAPL ) adding services such as Music and potentially web TV to strengthen its ecosystem . (Uncertainty) One area that AMZN could consider is the potential entry into wireless services by becoming an MVNO similar to Google ( NASDAQ : GOOG ) ( NASDAQ : GOOGL ) by offering Prime members an additional wireless service on AMZN 's own smartphone . (Uncertainty) This will not only allow higher ecosystem penetration but also would be accretive to AMZN 's hardware initiative . (Surprise) While I agree that the first Amazon phone was not as successful as expected , I believe that the added wireless service could be an attractive value proposition . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Recall that last quarter Amazon 's AWS sale was significantly higher than its second largest rival , Microsoft Azure , which had around $ 700m in sales last year . (Amazon: Surprise) More importantly , AMZN was able to achieve 14 % operating margin despite the competitive price war among the cloud service providers . (Surprise) Given that pricing appears to be stabilizing , I expect AWS to accelerate its growth this quarter and when AMZN ventures deep into the SaaS market via its WorkMail application and Internet of Things services . (Uncertainty) Looking at the long term, pricing will likely to come down so one differentiator is the level of services and customer relationship that a vendor establishes over the years.AMZN has been building relationships with developers who trust AWS's reliability, functionality and ease of use.With competition likely to increase in the long run , I believe that the relationships AMZN have accumulated will allow the company to ensure a stable market share .
