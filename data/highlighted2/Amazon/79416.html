Amazon 's Dirty Little Secret , Free Same Day Delivery Is n't Free - Amazon.com , Inc. ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) | Seeking Alpha Items shipped via same day Prime can cost more than items fulfilled by the standard Prime shipping . (Amazon: Surprise) Amazon seems to be targeting affluent areas that are less cost focused . (Amazon: Surprise & Uncertainty) It 's no surprise that Amazon 's ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) customers are embracing last week 's free same-day shipping announcement . (Amazon: Surprise) Who would n't want to spend more time with their family rather than run errands ? (Surprise) Time is a valuable commodity and many people are willing to pay a little extra for convenience which is good , because the cost of free shipping may be embedded in the price of the items . (Uncertainty) It 's not apparent though so consumers will likely spend more for products at the same time that Amazon builds brand loyalty . (Amazon: Surprise & Uncertainty) Over the years Amazon has been accused of ignoring profitability to gain market share but in this case , it looks like there will be little if any hit to margins . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Last week , Amazon began offering free same-day shipping to Prime subscribers in 14 major metropolitan areas , delivering products by 9PM if ordered by noon . (Amazon: Uncertainty) The areas receiving service seem to be focused on an income demographic more than geographic region . (Surprise) For example, the New York City is covered but not all of the boroughs, why Brooklyn ( home of Norah Jones and Anne Hathaway ) but not the Bronx ?If it was due to geographic issues , why include Hoboken New Jersey ? (Uncertainty) In my humble opinion , it 's because trendy places populated by young , affluent people are less price sensitive and more willing to pay up for convenience . (Surprise) So let's run a test on cost.The item I lose most when traveling, hands down, is my Bluetooth headset.I've donated several to the Metro North lost and found over the years not to mention airlines.Comparing the cost of a Plantronics Voyager Edge headset by buying through different parties on the Amazon platform reveals a wide variety of prices . (Amazon: Uncertainty) Through a third party (including shipping) the cost is $102.99 while it is $123.99 for Prime delivery but it jumps to $127.99 for Prime same day delivery.The difference between fulfilled by Prime and fulfilled by same-day Prime are $4.00 or 3%.Not a lot of difference to a consumer but when you consider that Amazon 's Free Cash Flow yield is only 2 % ( $ 1949 / $ 88988 ) it matters very much to the company . Ensuring that products fulfilled through prime same-day delivery embed the cost of shipping means that if this service is rolled out in a larger scale , margins will not degrade . (Surprise & Uncertainty) This is n't the first time that Amazon padded pricing to offset shipping costs . In February 2014 , a class action lawsuit was filed against Amazon for encouraging third parties to inflate the price of their items to cover shipping costs . The logic was that since Amazon charged for Prime membership , the cost of shipping was embedded in the membership price and by encouraging price inflation , Amazon was violating its own agreement that shipping would be free . The case went into arbitration in December 2014 which is considerably better than the judge finding in favor of the plaintiffs . (Surprise) If the financial claim had been substantial , offering `` free shipping '' could have opened the door to lawsuits but since it will be settled in arbitration , it should be considered a win for Amazon . (Amazon: Uncertainty) As this service becomes more widely rolled out , it would be nice to see price parity between Prime and same day Prime fulfilled items . (Surprise) However , investors can feel confident that this new service is not likely to degrade margins .
