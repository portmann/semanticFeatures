Amazon
It is truly remarkable for Jeff Bezos , Amazon 's founder and CEO , to have built the company to the size it is today ; but at 25 times book value and 970 times earnings , shares are priced for the coming of the `` Age of Amazon , '' a scenario in which profitability explodes in 2016 . If AMZN disappoints and profitability does not explode by 200 % in 2016 , the shares are ripe for a nasty fall , given their lack of valuation support .
