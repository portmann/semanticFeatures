Amazon
Compared to larger technology peers Amazon 's valuation still has room to double or triple in the event a larger share repurchase program becomes a reality over the next decade . The key focus for investors is understanding that the company will go through cycles of volatility and taking risk investing in Amazon during these lower levels provides for a good opportunity .
