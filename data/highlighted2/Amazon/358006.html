Amazon
Amazon 's intent to consider how the company will transition into the transportation services industry will benefit both top line and bottom line growth . Compared to larger technology peers Amazon 's valuation still has room to double or triple in the event a larger share repurchase program becomes a reality over the next decade . (Amazon: Surprise) The key focus for investors is understanding that the company will go through cycles of volatility and taking risk investing in Amazon during these lower levels provides for a good opportunity .
