Additionally , the company could bundle its own shows and offer them to consumers as subscription packages . (Uncertainty) As for the networks , they will get access to Amazon 's immense customer base and reach these younger viewers that are a sustainable platform . (Amazon: Surprise) Although Amazon 's OTT ambition is sound , it 's worth cautioning that its growing penetration could potentially pose a threat to rivals such as HBO ( NYSE : TWX ) , Hulu and Netflix ( NASDAQ : NFLX ) that are competing for almost the same clientele .
