Microsoft ( along with Alphabet and Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) ) is emerging as a leader in cloud services . The cloud-based Azure platform saw its revenues jump 135% last quarter.Revenues from the Intelligent Cloud division, at $5.9 billion last quarter, are quickly catching up to its Productivity and Business Processes division.This unit - which includes Microsoft Office - earned $6.3 billion last quarter.Though Microsoft stock yields only 2.7% at current prices, it has become something of a favorite among dividend investors due to MSFT's great track record of raising the dividend.In September, MSFT announced a 16% dividend hike, and the company has grown its dividend at a 19% annual clip over the past three years.Given that Microsoft has nearly $ 100 billion in cash and equivalents , I 'd say odds are good that growth will continue .
