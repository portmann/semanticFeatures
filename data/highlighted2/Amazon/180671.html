Amazon
Amazon gets paid up front in cash for services provided over time , and this inflates near-term cash flow . The company has to keep growing quickly to drive the negative cash cycle.While this certainly seems like a bear stance and does pose an appropriate risk to the company 's current valuation , we believe the negative cash cycle is sustainable given that there really is n't nor has there been a great deal of pressure from suppliers . (Uncertainty) As such , we believe the company should continue to produce operating cash flow in excess of mid-single-digit billions of dollars per year . (Uncertainty) With the AWS and Prime businesses looking as strong as ever, we think operating cash flow can grow over time as profitability and ROIC in each business grow.While AMZN is certainly not a risk-free investment here as a lot of moving parts must fall in place for the greater Amazon machine to operate fluidly , we do believe that the negative cash conversion cycle is sustainable , AWS will deliver high ROIC , and Prime is growing into a globally dominant business .
