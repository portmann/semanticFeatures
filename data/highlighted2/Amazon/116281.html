negotiating demands As a result the groups say they have already seen fewer titles published by major publishing houses each year While the groups contend that Amazons business practices hurt the cost in order to bring in business for its other businesses has driven retailers out of business In turn this has caused deflation across the industry and reduced the amount of revenue available for
