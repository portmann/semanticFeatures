All in all , Microsoft is a hungry # 2 that is aggressively going after the # 1 , Amazon . Microsoft's 2015 December quarter's (2016 Fiscal Q2) revenue and net income show the importance of Cloud to the company's future.While all the other segments had declining revenue YoY, the Cloud segment showed 5% growth despite the strong US dollar.This trend is likely to continue given the focus on Cloud as well as decline in traditional computing.Cloud right now represents 23% of the recent quarter's revenue.Assuming Microsoft 's Intelligent Cloud unit grows at a low 5 % to 6 % per year and the rest of the divisions decline at the current rate of 4 % to 5 % per year , we can see that the Cloud division is set to bring in bulk of the revenue in a few years ' time . (Uncertainty) Realistically , Cloud is likely to grow at a higher pace and the other traditional units will wane faster than assumed here . (Surprise & Uncertainty) We also like the fact that Microsoft is tightening up some loose ends by reducing its focus on less profitable segments and enhancing cloud spending .
