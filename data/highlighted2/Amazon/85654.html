Amazon is pulling product searches away and weakening brick and mortar stores that advertise on Google Sites . The race is on between Amazon and Google to see who can expand same-day or overnight delivery to more cities at a faster rate . (Amazon: Surprise) If Google can outpace Amazon , the company 's search and advertising growth may ramp back up .
