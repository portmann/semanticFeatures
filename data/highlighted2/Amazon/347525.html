The article I linked above goes on about how the new Fitbit Blaze is selling way better than the Apple Watch on Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) . Again, the Fitbit Blaze starts at $199, while the Apple Watch only after its recent price cut starts at $299.We're getting closer to an even comparison but we're not there yet.Additionally, the Blaze is a new product versus the Apple Watch which is basically a year old.Of course, Apple Watch sales are going to tail off a bit as consumers wait for the second edition to be launched.Had the Apple Watch launched in the past month or two, this would be a valid comparison, but again, we are comparing apples to oranges, no pun intended.So can we please stop with the invalid comparisons between Fitbit and the Apple Watch?Yes, Fitbit sells more units, but at a fraction of the price.Apple probably generated more Watch revenues in the last four months of 2015 than Fitbit did in the entire year .
