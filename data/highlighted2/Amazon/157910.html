As consumers increasingly rely on their smartphones and tablets for most personal computing needs, the living room is becoming the last refuge of the non-mobile personal computer.Integration with mobile devices will become an important discriminator, and here, Apple has the advantage.Apple TV will be accessible from any iOS device, as well as Apple Watch.Siri based control will also be available through any iOS device.The iOS Continuity feature will be extended to Apple TV as well.Tasks and apps that are started on an iOS mobile device will become activated on the Apple TV (if desired) just by entering the room.Previous generations of Apple TV were experiments, iOS devices that weren't quite the equal of even the iPhone.The next generation promises to become much better integrated into the iOS ecosystem, and play a pivotal role in the home.Other up and coming stars of the Apple pantheon might put in cameo appearances , such as the large ( 12-13 '' ) screen iPad Pro .
