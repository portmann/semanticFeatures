Amazon
There 's no other realistic alternative to capturing the Internet TV space aside from Amazon ( NASDAQ : AMZN ) . However , Amazon is not a pure play on Internet TV , and to Amazon , public cloud is a much bigger priority than Instant Video viewing hours . (Amazon: Surprise) Therefore , investors who want exposure into web TV do n't have a long list of candidate to choose from . (Uncertainty) Netflix secured huge wins in the early innings of the Internet TV space.The company signed a deal with Disney (NYSE: DIS ), and launched various in-house productions.This keeps the process of content bidding a bit more honest as Netflix can always revert to more in-house content to avoid price gouging . (Surprise) Netflix expanded internationally, so it will also have a global base of subscribers to market content to, which creates trouble for regionally locked content studios.The ability to source higher quality content becomes easier when you operate on a 30 % growth rate for revenues . (Surprise) MVPDs ( multichannel video programming distributors ) are n't growing at the same rate as Netflix , so eventually ; the competing economics of content deals for online streaming becomes better whereas deals with MVPDs become less desirable . (Surprise) When assuming a 30 % top line growth rate , Netflix 's revenue will reach $ 20 billion by 2020 . (Uncertainty) The growth will have to accelerate by 5 points, but I think that's feasible given the adoption rate of 4K TVs, and the robust growth in international subscribers (68.5% year-over-year growth in Q2 2015).The movement of domestic subscribers to higher pricing tiers will eventually materialize by 2017 . (Surprise) I also think international subscriber growth will be driven by China , as a wave of deregulation will make it easier for Netflix to launch its streaming service in the mainland . (Surprise) My long-run net income forecast is $ 3 billion on a 15 % profit margin assumption . (Uncertainty) The company may produce higher margins , but given the rapid investment into content and in-house productions , I 've tempered my expectations on margins . (Surprise & Uncertainty) If Netflix were to trade at perhaps 60 times earnings , the stock would be worth $ 180 billion . (Uncertainty) Even at 45 times earnings the stock would be worth $135 billion.The valuation will contract from 240 times earnings , but anticipating a more rapid reduction in valuation would be ridiculous .
