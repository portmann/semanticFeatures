Amazon
Amazon Web Services was one of the first major cloud infrastructure providers , and it is currently the biggest by a sizable margin . The company's aggressive approach in garnering market share has led to spectacular revenue growth.However , its slim margins aimed at undercutting the competition mean that AWS may not yet be profitable , at least according to Citi estimates .
