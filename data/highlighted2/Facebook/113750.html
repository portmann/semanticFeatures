Social Networks Unpacked Charts That Explain The Rise Of Facebook Twitter And Instagram Seeking Alpha The three most popular consumer social networks Facebook NASDAQ FB Twitter NYSE TWTR and Instagram have grown from scrappy upstarts to some of the most powerful communication platforms on earth Despite the similarities of these platforms users celebrities and companies are embracing them in different ways To understand how they are evolving and performing we researched engagement on the platforms across six user categories consumer products services enterprises news celebrities and governmental organizations The results revealed some interesting insights especially for Twitter and Instagram Consumer focused companies have gained unparalleled exposure to large audiences on Facebook As shown below the prominent brands in our survey average million Facebook followers compared to million on Twitter and million on Instagram The difference in the sizes of these platforms partly explains the difference in the number of followers for the brands Facebook has roughly five times the number of monthly users billion as Twitter million and Instagram million Even after adjusting for size however four times as many people follow consumer companies on Facebook than on Twitter or Instagram That said Instagram is growing more rapidly than Twitter and Nike BMW and Red Bull all have more followers on Instagram than Twitter Facebook also is the dominant platform for service companies but the scale of service companies presence on social networks across the platforms is significantly smaller than that of consumer brands While prominent companies that sell goods reach tens of millions of fans on Facebook companies that sell services reach an average of that figure as shown below Additionally Instagram has very little penetration for service companies most likely because services do not translate as easily into photos Social networks have much less penetration in the enterprise space As shown below despite their enormous market capitalizations companies such as IBM Merck and AIG reach only a few hundred thousand people on average on social media platforms In fact the total reach of the ten enterprise companies shown below is roughly the same as the number of Starbucks followers on Twitter alone The primary reason is that enterprise companies serve other companies as opposed to individuals Interestingly Twitter is punching above its weight in the enterprise category and along with LinkedIn NYSE LNKD it is the top platform used by sales and marketing people Facebook and Twitter both play important roles in the propagation of news reaching an average of eight and seven million newsoriented followers respectively as shown below Twitter is especially well positioned for real time news At BBC and CNN Twitter accounts are called BBC Breaking News and CNN Breaking News respectively while their Facebook counterpart uses standard branding While Twitter dominates breaking news stories Facebook is superior in news sharing Shareholic found that of web referral traffic comes from Facebook compared to from Twitter Buzzfeeds viral story about the infamous colordebated dress generated million viewers on Facebook but fewer than one million on Twitter Social media maximizes its potential in the hands of powerful celebrities often reaching tens of millions of people across multiple networks As shown below Facebook reaches the largest number of people but on average using the geometric mean Twitter is slightly ahead Instagram despite being a relative newcomer has strong momentum especially with young artists Twentyoneyearold Ariana Grande has more followers on Instagram than on Twitter or Facebook Celebrities appear to treat Twitter and Instagram as personal accounts and Facebook as a marketing platform They often push random thoughts selfies and informal videos to Twitter and Instagram Their Facebook feeds appear more curated and promotional with public relations and marketing more in control Fans have taken note and are gravitating toward the more authentic sources with engagement particularly high on Instagram Twitter dominates other social networks in the dispersion of realtime information from governmental and humanitarian organizations People turn first to Twitter for the latest White House announcement Center for Disease Control warning and Red Cross disaster relief efforts Facebook plays a less important role while Instagrams flattering filters arent as relevant in communicating fastbreaking serious situations Facebook Twitter and Instagram play distinct roles in connecting people with companies celebrities and news Facebook by virtue of its billionuser reach plays the biggest role Twitter though only a fifth of Facebooks size is the goto source for breaking news and the preferred way to connect with celebrities Most interesting is the rise of Instagram Just fiveyearsold it has strong presence among major brands and is the preferred platform for young celebrities With Facebook playing an increasing role in general news dissemination and celebrities flocking to Instagram Twitters stronghold remains realtime events If Twitter is to succeed as a platform it must succeed here Companies were selected from the Fortune and Fortunes Most Valuable Brands
