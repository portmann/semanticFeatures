looking for inspiration although the original looks just as amazing The batter for my cake includes coconut oil whipped cream with differences in measurements Instead of using wheat flour I opted for plump them up Any time I create a recipe I hope for the best because you really dont know how it will turn out Measurements for baking must be precise and although I know many of us get lucky at times there are those situations where a recipe will flop This apple cake turned out spectacular although I had too much batter for my cake pan which meant I made six large apple cake muffins as well If you have a larger cake pan and want to experiment with the baking time go for it otherwise kill two birds with one stone and make a cake and muffins with this apple cake recipe I baked the cake in the oven at degrees for about minutes I always test with a toothpick to make sure its clean before I remove cakes or muffins from the oven Once you take the cake from the oven poke holes in the top with a skewer or fork and pour the boiled heated condensed milk over the top along with a sprinkle of cinnamon and the butter rum soaked raisins Once cool you can cut into squares set on parchment paper in an airtight container and freeze up to months Take out piece by piece to thaw and it will take you back to the moment it came fresh from the oven and cooled I was shocked and every time I eat a piece of the apple cake Im still shocked that its so moist and never ever dry Yep thats magic if youre a cake baker This apple cake magically stays moist and fresh even after you freeze and defrost it A slice of this apple cake paired with a cup of coffee tea or cold glass of milk makes the perfect snack or dessert Grease a x cake pan and large muffin pan or inch cake pans This recipe will make a x cake AND large muffins both baked for minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean In a measuring cup add your buttermilk and to that add tablespoons of white vinegar Let it sit for about minutes or until curdled Stir and youre ready to use it If youre making homemade caramel see recipe link in post for Ree Drummonds easy caramel sauce which makes cups of caramel although you only need cup for this recipe You can use the rest as topping if youd like Otherwise storebought caramel is fine Sift together the flour baking powder baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl In your stand mixer add the coconut oil vegetable oil brown sugar and mix together Add in the homemade apple sauce which you can have sweetened or unsweetened but keep in mind the recipe is sweet already Add in the caramel vanilla extract and mix for about seconds Add in your eggs one at a time until combined Add in the buttermilk and whipping cream then mix until combined Little at a time add in your dry mixture that you sifted earlier until combined Pour into your cake pan and muffin pan or round pans Bake at degrees for minutes I like to check on my cakes around the minute mark and again at minute intervals Once it comes from the oven poke holes with a skewer on top of the cake and pour your warmed can of condensed milk over the top sprinkle with the butter rum raisins and add a bit of cinnamon if you like You can serve the apple cake as is or I like to top it with a bit of half and half cream or whipping cream Add this to your recipes for apple desserts so the next time you have a few extra apples
