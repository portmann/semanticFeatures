clicks or engagement As a result of this change some campaign reporting metrics related to clicks may look different By excluding likes shares and comments CPC may increase but will also become more valuable as it counts only the clicks you want Similarly it may look like your clickthrough rate NYSE CTR has decreased again this is because the CTR will no longer factor in the additional clicks All told though the actual cost and underlying efficacy of course will remain unchanged and I think that will be fairly transparent to those paying for them The platform is what the platform is But what
