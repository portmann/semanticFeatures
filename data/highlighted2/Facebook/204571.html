during sharp selloffs and keep your eyes on where the stocks are likely to be a few years down the road The selloff can be used to buy stocks that are on your watch list for a longterm investment I that now and see what a great buying opportunity that selloff created It was Barron Rothschild who was credited with saying The time to buy is when there is blood in the streets Rothschild made a lot of money by buying in the panic that followed the Battle of Waterloo So now that the market is in the red it is time to start looking at your buy list While fears are focused on a slowdown in China Apples stock has been punished However the people in China tend to have a strong love for American products and Apple products are among them Fellow author Mark Hibben recently pointed out that Apple is likely to continue to grow at a strong doubledigit pace in China I think that this is likely due to the love for the Apple brand among aspirational people in China I also think that the fear over Chinas slowdown is overdone Even if Chinas GDP slows to below in future quarters it is not likely to cause the wealthier citizens to stop buying Apples iPhones I also dont think that the yuan devaluation will make the iPhones too expensive for these aspirational citizens What typically holds true for momentum names with strong fundamentals is that the stocks are a buy after sharp selloffs Although Im not a fan of the valuations for Amazon Netflix and Tesla those stocks will probably bounce back significantly after the markets bleeding has stopped These three stocks have defied normal
