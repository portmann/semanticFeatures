Facebook eyeing TV dollars rolls out new ad products Reuters Related Stocks Markets Facebook Cyclical Consumer Goods SAN FRANCISCO Sept By Yasmeen Abutaleb SAN FRANCISCO Sept Facebook Inc introduced a slate of new advertising products on Sunday most of which are aimed at luring television advertisers onto the billion user social network The advertising options most of which will also be available on Facebookowned Instagram are designed to take advantage of the social networks strengths on mobile devices It has the worlds most popular smartphone app and generates more than threequarters of its billionplus in annual ad revenue on phones Facebook is trying to convince advertisers especially those who use video that their dollars will be better spent on mobile platforms rather than on TV as users especially millennials spend more time on their phones than watching television The rollout of the new products come ahead of New York Citys th Advertising Week which runs from Monday to Friday and gathers the worlds largest advertisers and companies Facebook also announced on Sunday that it has million active advertisers in total up from million in February Digital video advertising spending is growing rapidly projected to increase percent to nearly billion by according to eMarketer Television ad spending by comparison is expected to grow percent in the same time period to billion Facebook is listening to the ad community and giving them what they are looking for said Debra Aho Williamson social media marketing analyst with eMarketer Does Facebook want video ad dollars Yes On television advertisers can buy ads based on how many people they will reach an approach Facebook has adopted to ease the transition between television spending and digital spending In addition it can target highly specific audiences such as women aged to years old who have shopped on a specific website which TV cannot do Among the new products are brand awareness ads which aim to reach a large number of people to promote a companys name and brand such as Coca Cola Advertisers will also be able to poll users on mobile phones about whether they saw an ad a feature that used to be available only on desktop computers and they can use a format that allows them to display multiple videos at once that users can scroll through We want to be the singlemost important platform for all
