market cap well over billion Obviously the company doesnt want to risk all that it will continue leveraging its intellectual resources and data to generate new technological tools for others to use in the pursuit of creativity and running business models That still doesnt mean that Apple cant afford to spend some of its corporate time acquiring screenplays The Apple TV product helps to prove the point It is being positioned as an alternative to cable and its recent announcement that the companys ecosystem offers subscription access to HBO is notable HBO is a distributor of content produced by others but it also produces its own content In fact what is the value of the HBO brand today Is it based on reruns of an old s movie starring Molly Ringwald or is it tied more closely to Game of Thrones and Girls Shows like the latter have upgraded HBO in the minds of many consumers of entertainment and the proper curation of such content helps to differentiate a service Netflix is in a
