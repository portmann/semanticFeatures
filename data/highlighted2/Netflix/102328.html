offer live sports news or entertainment VZ could potentially offer a bridge between the current TV offerings and the more stale NFLX and Amazon NASDAQ AMZN offerings The US domestic market is a perfect example of the massive profits available in a duopoly or triopoly structure For over a decade both Verizon and ATT have delivered quality service and earned tremendous cash flows primarily at the expense of a bumbling Sprint NYSE S and a nonfocused TMobile NYSE TMUS Recently a racetothebottom mindset has plagued the industry as both Sprint and TMobile are offering budget plans free of the oldfashioned year contract system Although this price competition benefits consumers in the short term there is a risk of deterring investment in longterm infrastructure such as rural G and metro G if the profit potential begins to evaporate Just as a monopoly system as seen in Mexico for decades and in the US until the s is extremely inefficient a system of rampant competition spurned by shady to be a longterm mistake Giving the US three major telecom players will benefit both consumers and the shareholders of these three entities Consumers will benefit from quality infrastructure pseudo pricewar for retail mobile consumers I believe regulators will eventually come to their senses and allow what I believe to be an inevitable merger between Sprint and TMobile to occur This merger will allow more stability between the two legacy carriers and a more consumerfriendly TMobileSprint Consolidating the market into three strong players will actually improve legitimate longterm competition and innovation as Sprint certainly doesnt have the financial strength to live up to its infrastructure responsibilities and TMUS lacks the necessary scale to compete for the long term Consumers might temporarily win from the current price war but they will lose in the long term due to a reduction in longterm infrastructure deployment and innovation The FCC continues to offer
