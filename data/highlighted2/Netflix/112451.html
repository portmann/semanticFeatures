Should Netflix Add A Free Tier Netflix Inc NASDAQNFLX Seeking Alpha Netflix has repeatedly insisted that they will not include ads into their service We suggest that Netflix should consider adding a free tier with ads similar to Spotify Online video streaming giant Netflix NASDAQ NFLX has had significant coverage in recent years with equal amounts of hope and doubt on either side We believe that Netflix though unbelievably successful could heighten that success by considering an addition to its business model We believe that Netflix should switch its model toward a dualtiered model similar to the model offered by music streaming service Spotify Netflix should offer a free tier and a premium tier to its users The free tier would give a user full access to the Netflix library while introducing ads during playback The premium tier would be the exact same service that current Netflix subscribers receive We believe that Netflix should adopt such a model for two reasons First we believe that the introduction of a Netflix freetier would transform the video streaming market in Netflixs favor Currently Netflixs primary competitors in the streaming market are Hulu and Amazon Prime Neither of the two services offer free unlimited access to their library Hulu offers very limited access to their streaming catalog to free users Amazon Primes instant video library is only available to Prime subscribers In a switch to our proposed dualtiered model we believe that Netflix would offer the strongest video streaming product at both the free and paid pricepoints Furthermore we believe that the new model would steal free viewers from Hulus and piracys market share Second and most importantly we believe that the adoption of a freetier with ads would grow Netflixs revenues With the size of Netflixs user base Netflix would become one of the most indemand platforms for online advertisers While there are many different online advertising strategies we believe Netflix
