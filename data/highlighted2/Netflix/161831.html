acquisitions to create a valuable studio portfolio Theres been a lot of debate about the cordcutting phenomenon and how it will affect media companies like Disney NYSE DIS and Comcast NASDAQ CMCSA Netflixs continued evolution will be original programming and movie distribution Output deals and winning competitions for rights to older hit TV series wont matter as much over the long run if people dont feel that the service is searching for great stories to tell The company must also look to emphasize ownership of the programming it develops and to distribute it beyond Netflix Shareholder value can be enhanced in this manner and the business model can transform over time turning the streaming entity into a complete media conglomerate CEO Reed Hastings has already mentioned the importance of owning content for his company but he must place that as a top priority equal to that of growing the subscriber base In other words Netflixs grand scheme all along should be for it to become the next Disney the next Time Warner the next Comcast with a streaming service at the core of its business but with a desire to sell original content to other parties Lets imagine how this might work Netflix could develop the next great episodic series about the apocalypse Maybe instead of zombies or vampires its a plague of witches and warlocks that walk the countryside and pester the survivors Perhaps the idea was incubated as a comic book and then turned into a Walking Deadtype series and placed on cable It becomes a hit Netflix of course would own the exclusive streaming rights and would play episodes of the hypothetical show after a certain time period no different than what happens now The difference would be that unlike what happens with House of Cards and Orange Is the New Black it doesnt start with Netflix And thats okay Because Netflixs ultimate future isnt about it being a subscription service although that will always be important more so it is about Netflix becoming a bestinclass storyteller that will sometimes start its story on Netflix and sometimes start its story on a platform owned by some other media conglomerate Its no different than Disney making a television show for a competitor network such as CBS as opposed to making it for ABC The Disney analogy is very apt since some of that companys Marvel characters are showing up on Netflix If DisneyMarvel can supply Daredevil to Netflix one would have to assume that Netflix could supply content that it develops to ABC Hastings has also talked about entering the movie business and playing around with window strategies Netflix will eventually want to attempt to produce allout blockbusters on the line of Jurassic World To fully capitalize on such an asset Netflix will have to start a thriving consumer products division and then just like Comcast and Disney it will be obliged to enter the theme parks arena The company also could look at Disney as a template in another area the art
