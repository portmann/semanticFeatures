original TV content Plus Amazon has usually been at the forefront of many of these consumer trends But a great company doesnt necessarily make a great stock The chief complaint for many existing and potential Amazon shareholders is that by almost every metric the stock is wildly overvalued The justification for this valuation has always been that the company could be profitable at almost any time but is choosing instead to plow its income back into the business to grow domestically and globally And its worked Amazon now has almost a quarter billion customers and is not only the biggest online losses as firms plow money back into the business in the name of growth Amazon is in the middle of year of its continuous growth cycle with no real signs of slowing down While Amazon has a dominant position in ecommerce its ventures outside of that core business have been hit and miss Kindle sales are slowing down and the Kindle Fire has been a dud In fact Amazon is plowing more money into the business now than ever before The company is already sinking hundreds of millions of dollars into original programming content and has committed to spending another B to expand its presence in India Amazon is also trying to take on Alibaba NYSE BABA in China but so far has had limited success For the first time Amazon may have trouble generating the return on investment that it has become accustomed to Amazon has always traded on revenue instead of earnings Amazon is forecast to continue growing revenues in and another in so if that trend continues it should be good news for
