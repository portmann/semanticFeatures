point subscriber growth will slow down When this happens Netflix will need to raise prices to generate the free cash flow to justify its valuation Netflix NASDAQ NFLX reported results Wednesday that were once again well received by the market Despite weak free cash flow and earnings the stock was up Thursday This is mainly due to another huge jump in subscriber and revenue growth that blew away the companys internal estimates Any investors who have been following Netflix for a while shouldnt be surprised by this Netflixs stock price has traded on subscriber and revenue growth instead of traditional metrics such as earnings and free cash flow for years now The result is a company whose stock price looks insanely expensive using any traditional valuation metric you can think of Much of the bear case points towards this valuation and the underlying fundamentals to conclude that Netflixs stock price will inevitably collapse when subscriber growth slows and the market realizes that Netflix is little more than an extremely low margin media company that cannot actually generate cash flow for its investors This is absolutely a legitimate risk for investors in Netflix today but it is far from an inevitable conclusion to the NFLX growth story In my opinion it is just as likely that NFLX justifies its valuation and indeed continues to grow by utilizing its pricing power once the
