NYSE or NASDAQ All four FANG stocks are listed on the NASDAQ The SP index is capitalizationweighted based on the number of shares available for trading the float Movement in the prices of stocks in the SP with higher market capitalizations from the number of shares available for trading ie the float have a greater impact on the value of the index than do companies with smaller market caps Facebook is currently ranked th in market capitalization in the SP index Amazon is Google is and And Netflix is This shows that when these companies at the top move so does the overall market in many
