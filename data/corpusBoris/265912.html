Brazil's Rousseff to cut 2016 fiscal goal -sources | Reuters BRASILIA Dec 15 Brazilian President Dilma Rousseff will cut the government's key 2016 fiscal savings target to 0.5 percent of GDP with the option of lowering it to zero if projected tax revenues fail to materialize, two sources with direct knowledge of the matter told Reuters. The government had originally proposed a primary surplus target of 0.7 percent of gross domestic product (GDP).
