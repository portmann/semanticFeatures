Wall St. opens higher after consumer spending data | Reuters Markets | Mon Aug 3, 2015 9:36am EDT Wall St. opens higher after consumer spending data U.S. stocks opened slightly higher on Monday as data showed consumer spending in June recorded its smallest gain in four months, bolstering expectations the U.S. Federal Reserve may hold back on a rate increase. The Dow Jones industrial average .DJI rose 9.15 points, or 0.05 percent, to 17,699.01, the S&P 500 .SPX gained 1.39 points, or 0.07 percent, to 2,105.23 and the Nasdaq composite .IXIC added 9.11 points, or 0.18 percent, to 5,137.39.
