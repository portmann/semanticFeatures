Kazakhstan plans to narrow fiscal gap in 2016-18 | Reuters ASTANA Nov 25 Kazakhstan's parliament approved the 2016-18 budget on Wednesday, which aims to narrow the fiscal gap to 1.6 percent of gross domestic product next year and 1.0 percent in 2018. The Central Asian state, hit hard by the plunge in the price of oil, expects budget deficit to hit 3.0 percent of GDP this year.
