Tesla (NASDAQ: TSLA ) and SolarCity (NASDAQ: SCTY ) are wrapping up deal talks and could announce a definitive merger agreement before the end of the week. No one likes this deal... other than Elon. So it is happening. In fairness, he has made over $12 billion since he started doing stuff that I did not predict would work, so he is unlikely to stop now. Verizon (NYSE: VZ ) is buying the Yahoo! Verizon's Tim Armstrong said that, Combining Verizon, AOL and Yahoo will create a new powerful competitive rival in mobile media, and an open, scaled alternative offering for advertisers and publishers. The deal will probably close in the first quarter of next year. The buyer worked with Bank of America (NYSE: BAC ) and others; the target worked with Goldman (NYSE: GS ), JPMorgan (NYSE: JPM ) and others. Carmike (NASDAQ: CKEC ) recut their deal with AMC (NYSE: AMC ). CKEC holders get $33.06 in cash or 1.0819 AMC subject to proration. Carmike adjourned today's shareholder meeting.
