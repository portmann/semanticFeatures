Amazon challenges Etsy with handmade marketplace - Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) | Seeking Alpha Oct 8 2015, 04:13 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) has launched a marketplace for handcrafted goods, a move that takes direct aim at market leader ETSY . "We don't allow any mass manufacturing, we don't allow any factory-made products. All of these products have to be handmade and hand-manufactured," Amazon Marketplace vice-president Peter Faricy told Reuters. The new site, called Handmade at Amazon, went live at 3 a.m. ET with a lineup of over 80K items from 5K sellers in 60 countries.
