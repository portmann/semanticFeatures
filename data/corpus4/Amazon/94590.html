Oracle extends cloud offerings - Oracle Corporation (NYSE:ORCL) | Seeking Alpha Jun 23 2015, 04:01 ET | About: Oracle Corporation (ORCL) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Oracle (NYSE: ORCL ) is expanding its cloud-computing offerings, bringing the company into more direct competition with Amazon Web Services. "We're prepared to compete with Amazon on price," said Executive Chairman Larry Ellison in a webcast, after announcing that Oracle would offer online storage and capability for customers to run their applications entirely in the cloud.
