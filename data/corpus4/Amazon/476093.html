Amazon unveils its air force - Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) | Seeking Alpha Aug 5 2016, 02:03 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) will reveal its first branded cargo plane, Amazon One, at Seattle's SeaFair Air Show today, emblazoned with "Amazon" on its belly, "Prime Air" on its sides and the Amazon smile logo on its tail. The freighter jet is among a fleet of 40 leased from Atlas (NASDAQ: AAWW ) and Air Transport Services Group (NASDAQ: ATSG ) to improve a supply chain straining to keep pace with the retailer’s growing sales and swelling ranks of Prime members.
