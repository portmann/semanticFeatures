Facebook revenue jumps as mobile ad sales surge | Reuters April 22 (Reuters) - Facebook Inc reported a nearly 42 percent rise in quarterly revenue, helped by a jump in mobile advertising sales. Net income attributable to stockholders fell to $509 million, or 18 cents per share, in the three months ended March 31, from $639 million, or 25 cents per share, a year earlier. The world's largest internet social network said revenue rose to $3.54 billion from $2.50 billion. Revenue from advertising was $3.32 billion, a 46 percent rise from a year earlier.
