Google, Facebook deploy automation to block extremist content - Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ:GOOG) | Seeking Alpha Jun 26 2016, 05:31 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Some of the web's biggest destinations for watching videos, including YouTube ( GOOG , GOOGL ) and Facebook (NASDAQ: FB ), have quietly started using automation to remove extremist content from their sites, sources told Reuters . The technology looks for "hashes," a type of unique digital fingerprint that internet companies automatically assign to specific videos, allowing all content with matching fingerprints to be removed rapidly.
