Facebook, eyeing TV, bolsters live video | Reuters SAN FRANCISCO Facebook Inc on Wednesday rolled out new features for its live video product, a major challenge to Twitter Inc's Periscope live-streaming service, and Snapchat's video features. Facebook Live, which offers streaming video, added a host of features to make it easier for users to find and share clips from friends, celebrities and media companies, marking its biggest push yet into real-time video.
