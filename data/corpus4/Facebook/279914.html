Facebook's Oculus to take pre-orders for Rift from Wednesday | Reuters Related: Stocks, Markets, Cyclical Consumer Goods, Technology Jan 4 Oculus, the virtual reality company owned by Facebook Inc, said on Monday it would take pre-orders for Rift from Wednesday. The Rift comes bundled with the game Lucky's Tale and a multiplayer space combat game, EVE: Valkyrie, Oculus said in a blog post.
