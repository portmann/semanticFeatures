Messenger now has 11,000 bots on its platform - Facebook (NASDAQ:FB) | Seeking Alpha Jul 1 2016, 04:30 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Facebook (NASDAQ: FB ) now has over 11K bots and 21K developers building them on Messenger, the social networking giant's David Marcus told CNBC.
