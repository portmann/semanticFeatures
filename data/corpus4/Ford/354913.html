Ford announces round of minor recalls - Ford Motor Company (NYSE:F) | Seeking Alpha Mar 31 2016, 11:10 ET | About: Ford Motor Company (F) | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor Ford ( F +0.3%) recalls about 48K vehicles in North America under three separate actions. Recall #1: 5,336 Ford Explorer and Lincoln MKC SUVs in Canada after a problem with overheating block heaters was identified. Recall #2: 38K Ford Transit vans from model years 2014 and 2015 due to the positioning of certain air bags.
