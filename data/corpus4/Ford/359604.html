Ford pumps $167 million into South African factory - regional head | Reuters PRETORIA, April 5 Ford Motor Co will invest 2.5 billion rand ($167 million)to expand its factory in South Africa in order to build a new model that would be exported elsewhere on the continent, its regional head said on Tuesday. "This reinforces South Africa's position as a strategic export base for Ford," Jim Farley told a news briefing.
