Flight testing milestone for the 737 MAX - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha May 2 2016, 11:15 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Boeing's ( BA -0.4%) 737 MAX 8 has completed high altitude flight testing in Bolivia, marking the first international trip for the 737 MAX flight-test fleet. The fourth and final test aircraft will make its first flight in the coming weeks, and the program remains on track for its first delivery in the third quarter of 2017.
