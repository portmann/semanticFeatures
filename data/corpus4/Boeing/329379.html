Boeing names Caret as new head of defense and space unit - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Feb 24 2016, 19:10 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Carl Surran , SA News Editor Boeing (NYSE: BA ) selects Leanne Caret to take over as the new leader of its $30B defense and space unit beginning March 1, following the surprise retirement of Chris Chadwick. Caret, a 28-year Boeing veteran, currently heads the company's global services and support business, and previously served as CFO of the defense unit and as head of its helicopter operation. Chadwick has led Boeing's defense and space unit since December 2013, and formerly ran the company's military aircraft business; he will remain with the company until later this spring.
