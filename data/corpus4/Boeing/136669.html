ETF Hub Aug 3 2015, 04:23 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Iran is planning to buy up to 90 planes a year from Boeing (NYSE: BA ) and Airbus ( OTCPK:EADSY ) to revive its aging fleet, as European delegations head to the country in record numbers with expectations that trade sanctions will be lifted by the end of the year. Last month's nuclear accord does little to lift a raft of U.S. sanctions against the Islamic Republic, but civilian aircraft makers are an exception to the rule - a boon to companies like Boeing.
