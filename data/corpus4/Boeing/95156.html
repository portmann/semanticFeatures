ETF Hub Jun 23 2015, 16:48 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Carl Surran , SA News Editor Boeing (NYSE: BA ) names Dennis Muilenberg to become its new CEO effective July 1, succeeding James McNerney. McNerney, who has served as CEO since 2005, will continue as Chairman and work as an employee until he retires at the end of February 2016.
