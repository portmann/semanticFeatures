Boeing says airline requests for delivery changes at historic lows | Reuters NEW YORK, April 27 Boeing Co said on Wednesday that airline requests to change delivery dates of aircraft remained "well below historical average," a sign that low fuel prices have not significantly altered the outlook for the market.
