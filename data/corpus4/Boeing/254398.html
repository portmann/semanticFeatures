Boeing completes detailed design for 787-10 - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Dec 3 2015, 08:38 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Boeing (NYSE: BA ) has completed the detailed design for its longest of three 787 Dreamliners, two weeks ahead of schedule. Major assembly of the 787-10 will begin in 2016, followed by first flight in 2017 and first delivery in 2018.
