Intel profit falls 6.3 pct | Reuters Oct 13 Intel Corp reported a 6.3 percent fall in quarterly profit on Tuesday, weighed down by continued weak demand for chips used in personal computers. The company's net income fell to $3.11 billion, or 64 cents per share, for the third quarter ended Sept. 26, from $3.32 billion, or 66 cents per share, a year earlier. ( bit.ly/1k2L5TW ) Net revenue fell to $14.47 billion from $14.55 billion, the world's largest chipmaker said.
