ETF Hub Jan 19 2016, 12:53 ET | About: HP Inc. (HPQ) | By: Ryan Foster , SA News Editor HP Inc. ( HPQ -3.2%) released a new point-of-sale product solution, the HP RP9 Retail System using Intel ( INTC +0.4%) data protection technology. From the PR: “The innovative technology creates a secure transaction path that routes data directly from the point of entry to a bank's servers, encrypting the sensitive information throughout the process. The HP RP9 with Intel® DPT for Transactions isolates and protects credit/debit information and personal data from the moment a transaction is initiated to ensure customer security throughout the lifecycle of the transaction.” Additional features: 15.6" or 18.5" diagonal displays and 10-point multitouch screens. The integrated hardware and software product includes Intel Data Protection Technology for Transactions and promises end-to-end payment protection and security for non-payment customer data.
