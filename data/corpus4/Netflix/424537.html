Of the 62%, 18% responded that they often get news from social media, 26% said sometimes, and 18% said hardly ever. The leading social media platforms where users get their news are Reddit, where 70% of users get news, 66% of Facebook users, and 59% of Twitter users. From 2013, all platforms have grown the percent of users who receive news from the sites, with Facebook experiencing the biggest increase. When looking at each sites' total reach, and the proportion of users who get news on each site, Facebook leads the way - Facebook reaches 67% of US adults, and 44% get their news on the site. Twitter only reaches 16% of adults, but 9% get news from the platform.
