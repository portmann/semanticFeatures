Automatic albums populate based on time photos were taken and location. The service arranges the highest quality photos within these parameters accordingly. "On this day" will surface previously taken photos based on date, similar to a service provided by Facebook. Additional improvements include support for photo folders with large quantities of media in them and a Poké detector. This feature was built my Microsoft Research to automatically arrange Pokémon Go screenshots into the service while identifying, organizing and enabling search of 150 specific Pokémon.
