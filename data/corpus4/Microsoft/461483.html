Microsoft introduces business video service - Microsoft Corporation (NASDAQ:MSFT) | Seeking Alpha Jul 18 2016, 13:24 ET | About: Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) | By: Eric McCaffrey , SA News Editor Microsoft ( MSFT +0.8%)  launches Stream , a video product geared for internal business and commercial purposes. It is being reported as having a feel similar to consumer video offerings such as YouTube and Vimeo with like, comment and recommendation features although with added security necessary for enterprise use. Corporate VP of Business Intelligence Products Group James Phillips tells TechCrunch the service may support speech-to-text, advanced translations, face recognition and live streaming at some point in the future. Enterprises will have the ability to flexibly and securely manage video rights and access.
