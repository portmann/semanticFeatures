Microsoft, Volvo disclose driverless car deal - Geely Automobile Holdings Ltd. ADR (OTCMKTS:GELYY) | Seeking Alpha Nov 20 2015, 03:41 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Volvo ( OTCPK:GELYY ) has struck a deal with Microsoft (NASDAQ: MSFT ) that will see the two companies work together in developing driverless vehicles and give potential car buyers an interactive shopping experience using the latter's HoloLens headset. Customers who come into a Volvo showroom will be able to use HoloLens to view a car in 3D, change colors and see how many of its features work. Although details were scarce in other areas, the move highlights the trend for technology companies to jump into the autos space. Oh my.
