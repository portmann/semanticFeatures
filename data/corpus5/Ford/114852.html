ETF Hub Jul 13 2015, 04:45 ET | By: Yigal Grayeff , SA News Editor Ford (NYSE: F ), General Motors (NYSE: GM ) and Fiat Chrysler (NYSE: FCAU ) are today due to start talks  with the United Auto Workers (UAW) over a new contract for employees, with the WSJ reporting that the two-tier wage system is likely to be a main topic of discussion. The UAW will work to close the gap whereby more veteran workers receive $28 an hour while newer staff get $19. Profit-sharing and healthcare packages are also likely to be changed.
