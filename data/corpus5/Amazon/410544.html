Amazon's Luxembourg tax deal verdict coming up - Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) | Seeking Alpha May 20 2016, 04:50 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor EU state aid regulators aim to rule on Amazon's (NASDAQ: AMZN ) tax deal with Luxembourg by July, sources told Reuters , and it may order the country's tax authorities to recover about €400M in back taxes. The European Commission's decision will come after a near-two year investigation into whether a Luxembourg 2003 tax ruling for an Amazon subsidiary allows the firm to pay less tax there than other companies. Of course the Amazon subsidiary allows the firm to pay less tax there than other companies, that's the whole point of it. Amazon should be happy if the damage is limited to $400M euros; my most recent article cited estimates of the liability as being much higher.
