Amazon in line to sell e-books to NYC schools - Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) | Seeking Alpha Apr 19 2016, 03:38 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Technology companies have long coveted classrooms, attempting over the years to persuade schools to adopt their devices and specialized software, with mixed success. Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) this week will find out whether it will be accepted into the nation's largest school district. The online retailer is in line to be awarded a contract to sell e-books to New York City schools worth as much as $64.5M over five years.
