Amazon steps up European expansion plans - Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) | Seeking Alpha Jan 22 2016, 04:31 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) is stepping up its investment plans across Europe this year, employing several thousand more staff as it seeks to expand amid stricter scrutiny of its tax, privacy and employment records. Amazon, which is also seeking to develop its digital media, grocery delivery and cloud businesses, will publish fuller details of its European operations on Friday.
