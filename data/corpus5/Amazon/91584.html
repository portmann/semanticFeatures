ETF Hub Jun 19 2015, 04:11 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) has sealed a digital and print book deal with Penguin Random House, the last of the five major publishers to reach an agreement with the Web retailer following a dispute last year over e-book pricing and shared revenue. "We are still in business with Amazon...and will continue to be," Penguin Random House said in a statement.
