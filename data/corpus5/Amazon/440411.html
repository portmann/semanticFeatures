Amazon's streaming video is starting to gain traction - Netflix, Inc. (NASDAQ:NFLX) | Seeking Alpha Jun 22 2016, 17:48 ET | By: SA Eli Hoffmann , SA News Editor No one outside of Amazon knows how many people are streaming video from Amazon or how much video they are streaming. But industry observers do have a proxy: The internet usage numbers provided by Sandvine. Sandvine says Amazon’s video now accounts for 4.26 percent of prime-time internet usage in the U.S.. That’s up from 1.31 percent three years ago. Amazon’s numbers are still small compared to Netflix (NASDAQ: NFLX ) and YouTube (NASDAQ: GOOG ), which continue to account for half of all broadband usage.
