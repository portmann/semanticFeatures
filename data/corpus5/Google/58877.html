ETF Hub May 13 2015, 20:10 ET | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor Avengers: Age of Ultron debuted in China with a $34M opening , Disney's (NYSE: DIS ) best ever in that country, and brought the film's global total to $932M. The Wednesday opening also provided the biggest non-weekend take in the country's history and accounted for a full 95% of box-office revenues there. Among other landmarks for the film's Chinese debut, it pushed Disney over the $2B global box-office mark for the company's 14th straight year. With moviegoers in China and Japan just joining in, the film's headed for the $1B club in the coming week, even if its subtitles are causing wild confusion with some Chinese moviegoers, who joked that the work seems to have been done by Google Translate rather than a pro.
