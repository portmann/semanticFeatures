(AAPL) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Apple (NASDAQ: AAPL ) is cleaning up its iOS App Store to remove malicious iPhone and iPad programs following what is being described as the first large-scale attack on the mobile software outlet. The hackers took aim at popular Chinese apps by convincing developers of legitimate software to use a tainted, counterfeit version of source code known as XcodeGhost. According to cyber security firm Palo Alto Networks, "hundreds of millions" of users are at risk of having their personal data exposed due to the malware.
