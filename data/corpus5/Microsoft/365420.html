A bigger one could be Netflix (NASDAQ: NFLX ). Disney pushes a ton of movie and TV content through Netflix. So far, Disney has failed to succeed at replicating Netflix's ability to get content directly to consumers. Disney's ESPN could be particularly valuable for Netflix while Netflix's Reed Hastings could be a particularly needed executive for Disney.
