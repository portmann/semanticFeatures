ETF Hub Dec 23 2015, 12:54 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Carl Surran , SA News Editor Boeing ( BA +0.7%) says it booked orders for 166 commercial jets in the past week, all for the company’s best-selling 737 family of jets. Boeing has now booked deals for 743 jets through Dec. 22 (excluding 100 cancellations), after targeting matching net new orders and deliveries this year and forecasting 750-755 jets would be delivered.
