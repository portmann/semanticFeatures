ETF Hub Jul 29 2015, 02:32 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor SpiceJet is in talks with Boeing (NYSE: BA ) and Airbus ( OTCPK:EADSY ) to buy more than 100 single-aisle jets worth about $11B at list prices, in what would be the Indian budget carrier's largest-ever plane order. A deal for the Airbus A320neo and Boeing 737 Max aircraft could be signed during the current financial year, which ends March 31.
