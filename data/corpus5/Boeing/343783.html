Gogo, Air Canada extend deal to cover international fleet - Gogo (NASDAQ:GOGO) | Seeking Alpha Mar 21 2016, 12:27 ET | About: Gogo (GOGO) | By: Jason Aycock , SA News Editor In-flight connectivity firm Gogo ( GOGO +2.7%) and Air Canada have extended their partnership to cover the airline's international fleet . Gogo will install its 2Ku technology on Air Canada's wide-body international craft starting with Boeing 777s this fall, and eventually including Boeing 787s.
