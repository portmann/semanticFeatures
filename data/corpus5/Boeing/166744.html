ETF Hub Sep 9 2015, 03:51 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yigal Grayeff , SA News Editor A British Airways Boeing 777 (NYSE: BA ) caught fire late yesterday as it was taking off from Las Vegas' McCarran International Airport. The pilot was able to abort the takeoff and all 172 passengers and crew escaped, although 14 people were taken to hospital. A preliminary investigation shows that the plane's left engine, which was made by GE (NYSE: GE ), burst into flames as the plane started to accelerate on the runway.
