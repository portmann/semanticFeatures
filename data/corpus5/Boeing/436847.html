Iran-Boeing deal said to involve 100 airliners - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Jun 19 2016, 08:19 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Iran has reportedly reached an agreement to buy 100 planes from Boeing (NYSE: BA ), pending the final authorization from U.S. Treasury officials. Until now, Boeing has only been granted permission to present its products to Iranian airlines as it tries to catch up with rival Airbus, which won a provisional deal earlier this year for 118 jets worth $27B.
