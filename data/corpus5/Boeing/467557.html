Report: Malaysia Air may announce Boeing order this week - The Boeing Company (NYSE:BA) | Seeking Alpha Jul 25 2016, 03:41 ET | About: The Boeing Company (BA) | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Malaysia Airlines may soon announce a plane order of Boeing (NYSE: BA ) B737 MAX jets, Business Times reports , as the airline eyes a fleet expansion by adding long-range narrow body aircraft. The carrier may place an order that could be up to half the size of a similar deal by Vietjet Air, which purchased 100 Boeing 737 MAXs worth $11.3B in May.
