ETF Hub Jan 28 2016, 08:52 ET | By: Eric Jhonsa , SA News Editor Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN ) is up 3.7% premarket, Google/Alphabet ( GOOG , GOOGL ) up 2.2%, and Netflix (NASDAQ: NFLX ) up 2.5% after fellow FANG trade member Facebook (up 14.7%) trounced Q4 estimates on the back of 57% Y/Y ad revenue growth . Nasdaq futures are up 0.6%. Separately, eBay, which has been losing share to Amazon and others for some time, is down 12.1% after issuing soft Q1/2016 guidance to go with in-line Q4 results . eBay's GMV was flat Y/Y at $21.9B, with Marketplace GMV dropping 3% to $20.7B. eBay spinoff PayPal is up 4.8% after beating Q4 estimates with the help of a 23% Y/Y increase in payment volume to $81.5B, and issued in-line Q1/2016 guidance. Amazon's Q4 report arrives this afternoon, and Google's on Monday afternoon. Last week, Netflix reported mixed Q4 results and strong subscriber adds .
