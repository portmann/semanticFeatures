ETF Hub Nov 30 2015, 03:26 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor The euro is headed for a steep monthly decline as economists unanimously forecast the ECB will unveil additional stimulus this week. The currency has weakened 3.8% in November, its biggest loss since a 4.2% decline in March, when the central bank embarked on a €1.1T asset-purchase program. In contrast, the U.S. Federal Reserve is widely expected to hike interest rates at its mid-December policy meeting, sending up the dollar index to 100.23, its highest level since mid-March.
