Tsipras: Greece will keep its word - Global X FTSE Greece 20 ETF (NYSEARCA:GREK) | Seeking Alpha Mar 13 2015, 04:52 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Despite a war of words between Athens and Berlin the last few days, Greek PM Alexis Tsipras looked to reassure his euro zone creditors on Thursday, saying he would stick to their extended bailout agreement and implement agreed upon reforms. "There is no reason for concern...even if there is no timely disbursement of a (loan) tranche, Greece will meet its obligations," he told reporters. The words sharply contrasted to the tone of the recent recrimination between Greece and Germany over austerity, relations between their finance ministers and World War II reparations.
