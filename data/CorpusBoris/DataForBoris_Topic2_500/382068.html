Greek creditors weigh extra austerity measures - Global X MSCI Greece ETF (NYSEARCA:GREK) | Seeking Alpha Apr 17 2016, 09:12 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Greece's creditors are considering seeking extra austerity measures that would be triggered if Athens misses its fiscal targets, in a bid to bridge differences between Europe and the IMF over bailout requirements. Under the proposal, Greece would have to sign up to so-called contingency measures of up to €3B, on top of the €5B package made up of tax increases and spending cuts the country and its lenders are already negotiating.
