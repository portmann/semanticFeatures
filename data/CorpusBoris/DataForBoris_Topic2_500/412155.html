Greek parliament approves austerity reforms - Global X MSCI Greece ETF (NYSEARCA:GREK) | Seeking Alpha May 23 2016, 02:54 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor Greece's parliament has approved a raft of fresh taxes and austerity measures needed to unlock further rescue loans, as the country's most influential creditors - Germany and the IMF - remain deadlocked over debt relief. "Greeks have already paid a lot, but this is probably the first time that the possibility of these sacrifices being the last is so evident," Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras told lawmakers.
