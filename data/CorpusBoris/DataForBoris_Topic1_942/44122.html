Gross: June rate increase is off | Seeking Alpha Apr 30 2015, 04:13 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor "I think they're going to raise rates once in 2015, if only because they want to prove that they can do it," Bill Gross said in an interview, stressing the Fed's possible increase in June is definitely "off." Yesterday, the FOMC brushed off a Q1 slowdown as weather-related and transitory in its policy statement, but gave no indication whether a June rate hike is off the table.
