25% of its assets in foreign securities.The large cap growth mutual fund returned 11.5% in the last one year.Brown Advisory Growth Equity Advisor (MUTF: BAGAX ) seeks capital growth.The fund invests a year.Columbia Large Growth Quantitative Fund A (MUTF: RDLAX ) invests a large portion of its assets in large cap companies having market capitalizations similar to those listed in the Russell 1000 fund held 79 issues with 8.14% of its assets invested in Apple Inc.
