Maxpoint IPO Could Be Right On Target - MaxPoint Interactive (Pending:MXPT) | Seeking Alpha MXPT, a digital marketing company specializing in hyperlocal advertising, is set to IPO on NYSE on Friday, 3.6.2015.Lead underwriters include Goldman Sachs, Deutsche Bank, and Pacific Crest.MXPT has posted impressive growth figures, albeit accompanied by increasing losses.MXPT has demonstrated its impact with companies such as Starbucks and Barilla.Maxpoint (Pending: MXPT ), a digital marketing company specializing in hyperlocal advertising, filed with the SEC for a $75 million IPO, set to price Thursday night, 3.5.2015.6.5 million shares are to be offered at a price range of $10.50 to $12.50 per share.Goldman Sachs, Deutsche Bank, and Pacific Crest are the book runners, with William Blair and Needham rounding out the syndicate.Originally the sole province of government agencies, big data is now highly noticeable in advertising.Maxpoint Interactive, headquartered in Morrisville, NC, specializes in cloud-based big data applications that cater to major national and international brands, wishing to refine their marketing to specific neighborhoods.By utilizing digital intelligence to cull social media, statistical trends and other criteria, Maxpoint has divided the USA into 44,000 "Digital Zips" or Zip Codes, through which its clients, including Starbucks (NASDAQ: SBUX ), Barilla Pasta and Electrolux ( OTCPK:ELUXY ), among others, are able to maximize their advertising budgets when opening a new store or launching a new product.Maxpoint's seven general client sectors are: Health Care, Retail, Restaurants, Entertainment, Automotive, Telecommunications, and Consumer Products.Their cloud based Maxpoint Intelligence Platform is sold either directly to customers or through advertising agencies.The founders of Maxpoint have an interesting pedigree: CEO Joe Epperson is a former Finance Director at eBay's (NASDAQ: EBAY ) PayPal; Gretchen Joyce was previously with Tesla Motors (NASDAQ: TSLA ), and Kurt Carlson was with NetQoS Inc.From a growth perspective, Maxpoint almost doubled its workforce personnel from roughly 170 to over 320 and increased 2014 revenues by 61% to surpass $100 million, vs. $66.1 million in 2013.However, the surge in revenue was accompanied by increased losses, from $187,000 in 2013 to $13 million in 2014.Enterprise Customers increased 58% to 479, which helped Maxpoint to realize a 2.5% increase in revenue per customer as well in 2014.Maxpoint's private equity investors include Trinity Ventures (32%), Madrona Venture Group (23.5%), and affiliates of Performance Equity Management (16%).Maxpoint has been able to tangibly demonstrate the advantages that the Maxpoint Intelligence Platform delivers to its clients.Barilla used Maxpoint software to test a new product launch and in-store sales increased over 20% vs. 100 stores that were not part of the campaign.Additionally, Maxpoint is a member of the Triangle Interactive Marketing Association (TIMA), which boasts a wide range of interactive digital design and marketing professionals that has had six members launching successful IPOs in 2014.Maxpoint will be the first TIMA member with an IPO in 2015.Given that the IPO calendar is relatively sparse at the moment, Maxpoint has been fortunate in its timing and could garner additional attention this week.We suggest
