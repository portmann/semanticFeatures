U.S. to push for greater fiscal spending at G20 | Seeking Alpha Feb 23 2016, 03:59 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor The United States will call on G20 countries this week to use fiscal policy in order to boost global demand, a senior Treasury official told Reuters ahead of a G20 meeting later this week in Shanghai. While there, American officials will also urge all members to refrain from manipulating exchange rates for competitive purposes, in line with existing G20 commitments.
