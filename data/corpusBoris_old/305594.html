Chile posts third straight annual fiscal deficit in 2015 | Reuters SANTIAGO Jan 29 Chile posted a fiscal deficit equivalent to 2.2 percent of estimated gross domestic product in 2015, the third year in a row it posted negative numbers, the government's budget office reported on Friday. It posted a fiscal deficit of 1.6 percent in 2014. Chile is the world's No.1 copper producer and government revenues have been hit as prices for the industrial metal languish at multi-year lows.
