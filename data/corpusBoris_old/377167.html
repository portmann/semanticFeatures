China Jan-Feb fiscal expenditures up 12.0 pct yr/yr | Reuters BEIJING, March 16 China's fiscal spending increased 12 percent in the first two months of 2016 from the same period of last year, the finance ministry said on Wednesday, quickening from a 10.5 percent gain seen in the same period a year ago. Growth in fiscal income accelerated to 6.3 percent in the January-February period, compared with a 3.2 percent rise in the same period of 2015.
