Brazil's Barbosa says fiscal recovery hinges on economic stability | Reuters BRASILIA Dec 17 Brazilian Planning Minister Nelson Barbosa said on Thursday that the government needs to stabilize a contracting economy in order to bolster fiscal savings next year. Barbosa said that is why the government proposed a lower 2016 primary surplus target as a way to maintain public investments and support the economy.
