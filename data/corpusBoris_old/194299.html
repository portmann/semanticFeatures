Brazil's Rousseff confident Congress will uphold spending vetoes | Reuters BRASILIA Oct 7 Brazilian President Dilma Rousseff expressed confidence that lawmakers will uphold on Wednesday her vetoes of two bills that would raise government spending in coming years. "I am sure that Congress will today show its commitment to Brazil," she said in a radio interview. "It's impossible for a country that is facing difficulties to disproportionately increase spending."
