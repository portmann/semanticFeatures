Brazil readies 65 bln reais of new spending cuts, revenue -sources | Reuters BRASILIA, Sept 14 President Dilma Rousseff's administration will announce additional spending cuts and revenue increases of 65 billion reais ($16.9 billion) on Monday to plug next year's budget deficit, two sources in the government said. The measures are aimed bridging a 30-billion real shortfall in the 2016 budget that Rousseff sent to Congress and raise revenues to meet a primary budget surplus of 0.7 percent of GDP.
