G20: Monetary policy alone can't bring balanced growth | Seeking Alpha Feb 28 2016, 08:45 ET | By: Yoel Minkoff , SA News Editor "Monetary policies will continue to support economic activity and ensure price stability...but monetary policy alone cannot lead to balanced growth," leaders from the world's top economies declared in a joint communique on Saturday following a two-day conference in Shanghai. "We will use all policy tools - monetary, fiscal and structural" G20 policymakers further exclaimed, while IMF Managing Director Christine Lagarde emphasized "a sense of renewed urgency," but cautioned that there wasn't too "much time left." Participants also repeated previous pledges not to engage in competitive currency devaluations and promised to "consult closely" on exchange markets.
