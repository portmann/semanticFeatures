Argentina says Sept fiscal deficit was 6.5 bln pesos | Reuters BUENOS AIRES Nov 24 Argentina's fiscal deficit was 6.5 billion pesos ($671.8 million) in September and 7.0 billion pesos ($724.9 million) in August, the economy ministry said in a statement on Tuesday.
