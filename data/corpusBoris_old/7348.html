Schaeuble says monetary policy no substitute for fiscal policy | Reuters BERLIN, March 18 (Reuters) - German Finance Minister Wolfgang Schaeuble reiterated his view on Wednesday at a news conference that he and European Central Bank President Mario Draghi are of the same opinion that monetary policy cannot be a substitute for fiscal policy.
