Consumer spending on services on the rise - Six Flags Entertainment Corporation (NYSE:SIX) | Seeking Alpha Mar 25 2016, 09:40 ET | By: Clark Schultz , SA News Editor Consumer spending rose 2.4% in Q4, an upward revision from the 2.0% pace first reported by the Commerce Department ( overall GDP was revised to 1.4% from 1.1% ). Some analysts think a bit of a pure-play bet on consumer spending is the theme park sector which is just now ramping up for the busy season. Per-capita spending could improve this year for Six Flags (NYSE: SIX ), Cedar Fair (NYSE: FUN ), and SeaWorld Entertainment (NYSE: SEAS ) after Disney (NYSE: DIS ) raised the bar on ticket prices.
