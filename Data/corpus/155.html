ETF Hub Mar 12 2015, 13:31 ET | About: CSOP FTSE China A50 ETF (AFTY) | By: Carolyn Pairitz , SA News Editor The CSOP FTSE China A50 ETF (NYSEARCA: AFTY ) covers the 50 largest A-shares companies whose shares are listed on mainland China stock exchanges in either Shanghai or Shenzhen. CSOP also manages a Hong Kong-listed ETF by the same name as AFTY; the fund has returned over 60% to investors since its launch in August 2012. "The listing of the CSOP FTSE China A50 ETF on the NYSE Arca offers U.S. investors access to a transparent, efficient, and liquid solution through which they can participate in China's exciting success story," commented Ms. Ding Chen, CSOP's Chief Executive Officer, in a press release .
